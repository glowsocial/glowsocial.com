**What is the best alternative to Marky?**
If you're comparing Marky and Glow Social, you've already figured out the most important thing: you need AI to handle your social media, not just schedule it. The real question is how much of the process you want to manage yourself.

Marky is a capable AI content tool. It generates posts from your website, supports multiple platforms, and has a design editor. But it's still a tool — **you drive it.** You generate batches of posts, scroll through them, queue the ones you like, manage your content calendar, and check your analytics.

Glow Social takes the work away entirely. We read your website, generate content pillars, create posts with captions and images, and publish them on your behalf. **You approve once a month. That's it.**

## Both Tools Use AI. Only One Removes the Work.

Marky and Glow Social start from the same place: you enter your website URL, and AI creates social media content tailored to your business. That's where the similarities end.

With Marky, you still need to:
- **Generate posts in batches** and scroll through them
- **Queue and schedule** each post yourself
- **Manage your content calendar** manually
- **Log in regularly** to keep your pipeline full
- **Choose and customize designs** for each post

With Glow Social, your only job is:
- **Approve posts once a month** (or let auto-scheduling handle it entirely)

That's the difference between an AI assistant and an AI service. One helps you do the work. The other does the work.

## What Marky Does Well

Let's be honest — Marky is not a bad product. It's earned 100K signups and strong reviews on Capterra and G2 for good reasons:

- **Lower entry price** — Their Solo plan at $39/mo is $10 less than Glow Social
- **Design editor** — You can customize post visuals in their built-in editor
- **Agency features** — Multi-workspace management for agencies handling multiple brands
- **Free trial** — 7-day free trial to test the platform before you pay

If you're a marketer, social media manager, or agency owner who enjoys the creative process and wants AI to speed it up, Marky is a solid choice.

## What Marky Doesn't Do

But if you're a local business owner — a roofer, dentist, salon owner, or coach — **you're not looking for a faster way to do social media. You're looking for a way to stop doing it entirely.**

That's where Marky falls short:

- **You still have to generate and manage batches.** Nobody's posting for you while you're on a job site.
- **No auto-publishing workflow.** You queue posts, but you're the one keeping the pipeline full.
- **Review monitoring is locked behind the $229/mo Pro plan.** For a local business, that's not just expensive — it means the most important local SEO feature requires a 6x price jump from Solo.
- **Fewer platforms.** Marky supports 7 platforms. Glow Social publishes to 12+, including Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, YouTube, Discord, and Slack.
