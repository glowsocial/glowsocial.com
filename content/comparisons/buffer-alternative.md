Buffer is one of the most popular social media scheduling tools for a reason: it's clean, reliable, and priced for individuals and small teams. But "scheduling tool" is the key phrase — and it's the thing most people miss when they're evaluating it.

## What Buffer Does

Buffer lets you:
- Schedule posts to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, Pinterest
- Manage multiple brand workspaces
- View basic analytics per post
- Use the AI assistant to refine captions you've already written
- Collaborate with a small team on drafts

It does all of that well. Buffer is a genuinely good product.

## What Buffer Doesn't Do

Buffer doesn't write your captions. It doesn't create your images. It doesn't come up with ideas. And it doesn't post anything until you tell it exactly what to post.

The bottleneck for most small business owners isn't publishing. It's **creating something worth publishing in the first place** — 12 times a month, indefinitely, while also running a business.

## When Buffer Is the Right Choice

Choose Buffer if:
- You enjoy creating content and want a reliable, affordable way to schedule it
- You have a team member or VA who handles content creation
- You're a creator with an active content workflow who just needs publishing handled
- You want a free plan to start with no cost

## When Glow Social Is the Right Choice

Glow Social is a done-for-you social media service that automatically creates and publishes posts for local businesses — $99/month, no marketing team required.

Choose Glow Social if:
- You don't have time to create 12+ pieces of content every month
- Content creation is the reason you're not posting consistently
- You want social media to feel like it's handled, not just scheduled
- You've tried scheduling tools and still end up with silent accounts

## What "Free" Actually Costs

Buffer's free plan covers 3 channels and 10 posts per channel. That's genuinely useful. But the cost of filling that queue is 15–20 hours of content creation every month.

At $75/hour for your time, that's $1,125–$1,500/month in opportunity cost to fill a free tool.

Glow Social at $99/month includes that content creation. The math isn't close.
