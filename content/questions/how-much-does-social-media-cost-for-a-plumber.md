# How much does social media cost for a plumber?

## Direct Answer
Social media for a plumbing company usually costs $0 plus owner time for DIY, $15-$100/month for scheduling software, $300-$1,500/month for a freelancer, $1,500+/month for an agency, or $99-$299/month for done-for-you automation. The real cost depends on whether the plumber needs publishing, content creation, strategy, or all three.

## The Hidden Cost
Plumbers make money in the field, not by sitting at a desk writing captions. If social media takes four hours per week, that is time that could have gone to estimates, service calls, follow-up, or operations.

## What Plumbers Should Pay For
The highest-value content for plumbers is usually practical and trust-building: drain tips, water heater warnings, leak prevention, reviews, service-area posts, and Google Business Profile updates.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The cheapest software is not the cheapest solution if the owner still has to create every post.
