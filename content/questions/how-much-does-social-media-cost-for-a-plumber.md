# How much does social media cost for a plumber?

## Direct Answer
Social media for a plumbing company usually costs $0 plus time for DIY, $15-$100 per month for scheduling software, $300-$1,500 per month for a freelancer, $1,500+ per month for an agency, or $99-$299 per month for done-for-you automation.

## Why This Matters
Plumbers make money in the field, not by sitting at a desk writing captions. Time spent creating posts has an opportunity cost.

## Real-World Example
If a plumber loses two billable hours per week to content work, the "free" DIY option can cost more than outsourcing.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The problem is usually not posting technology. It is creating useful, local, trust-building content every week.
