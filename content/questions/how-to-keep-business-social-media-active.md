# How do you keep business social media active?

## Direct Answer
Keep business social media active by using a repeatable weekly mix: one educational post, one proof post, and one service reminder. Batch content monthly or use a done-for-you system if the team cannot maintain it.

## Why This Matters
Consistency is usually an operational problem, not a creativity problem.

## Real-World Example
A plumber can repeat the same structure every week with different topics: prevention tip, review, service reminder.

## What Most People Get Wrong
You do not need a new campaign every week. You need a sustainable rhythm.
