# What should a restaurant post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A restaurant should post menu features, specials, events, reviews, staff picks, behind-the-scenes prep, catering reminders, and timely reasons to visit.

## Why This Matters
Restaurants compete for immediate decisions. A good post gives hungry customers a reason to choose you today.

## Real-World Example
A Friday post with a strong food photo, special, hours, and reservation link is more useful than a generic branding graphic.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Restaurants often post flyers instead of appetite. Real food photos and simple calls-to-action usually work better.
