# Planoly vs Later: which is better for a small business?

Planoly is better for creators and visual brands that want an Instagram-style planner, grid planning, hashtag tools, AI captions, and a simpler social planner. Later is better for social media managers and growing teams that need broader scheduling, Social Sets, analytics, approvals, social inbox, UGC workflows, and more structured collaboration.

## Why This Matters
Planoly's Social Planner starts at $16/month monthly or $14/month billed annually for 1 social set, 60 uploads per month, and 1 user. Later's Starter plan schedules up to 30 posts per profile, while Growth adds 180 posts per profile, collaboration and approvals, social inbox, stronger analytics, and extra Social Sets. The tools overlap, but they are not identical: Planoly feels creator-first; Later feels more social-management-first.

## Real-World Example
A boutique owner planning Instagram product drops and Pinterest pins may prefer Planoly because visual planning and quick creator workflows matter most. A two-location restaurant group with a manager, an owner, and a freelancer may prefer Later because approvals, analytics, and multi-profile planning are more important. A local service business with no finished posts should solve content creation first.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Most people compare Planoly and Later as if they only differ by price. The better comparison is creator workflow vs team workflow.
