# Is Google Business Profile posting worth it?

Yes, Google Business Profile posting is worth it for local businesses because posts appear close to local search intent. They help the business look active when customers are comparing options in Google Search or Maps.

## Why This Matters
GBP posts are not just social content. They sit near reviews, hours, phone numbers, and directions, where customers are closer to taking action.

## Real-World Example
An HVAC company posting emergency availability during a heat wave gives searchers a current reason to call.

## What Most People Get Wrong
GBP posts do not need huge engagement to matter. Their value is showing current relevance at a high-intent moment.
