# How to set up a Google Business Profile for free

## Direct Answer
Go to business.google.com, click "Manage now," enter your business name and category, add your address (or set a service area if you're mobile), verify by postcard or phone, then fill out every field — hours, services, description, photos, and attributes. Verification takes 3-7 days by mail. The profile is completely free and is the single most important local marketing asset most businesses underutilize.

## Why This Matters
Google Business Profile is the #1 local search ranking factor. When someone searches "plumber near me" or "best salon in [city]," Google pulls results from Business Profiles, not websites. A complete profile with photos, reviews, and accurate hours appears in the Map Pack — the 3-business box at the top of local search results. Businesses in the Map Pack get 70% of the clicks. Businesses below it fight over the remaining 30%. The difference between showing up and not often comes down to profile completeness. Google rewards profiles that fill in every available field — business description, services, products, attributes, and at minimum 5 photos.

## Real-World Example
A solo accountant set up her Google Business Profile but left the description blank, added no photos, and skipped the services section. She ranked on page 2 for "accountant in Mesa, AZ." After completing every field — a keyword-rich 750-character description, 12 office and team photos, all service categories, and business attributes — she moved into the Map Pack within 45 days. No ads, no SEO agency. Just a complete profile.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Most businesses create a Google Business Profile and stop at verification. That's like building a storefront and forgetting to put up a sign. The business description alone supports up to 750 characters of keyword-rich copy — and most profiles leave it blank. A social media setup tool can generate an optimized description from your website in seconds, but even writing one manually takes 10 minutes and dramatically changes your local search visibility.
