# Is hiring a social media manager worth it?

## Direct Answer
Hiring a social media manager is worth it if you need custom strategy, hands-on creative work, community management, or campaign planning. If you mainly need consistent posting, done-for-you automation may be more cost-effective.

## Why This Matters
Social media managers can be valuable, but many small businesses do not need the full scope they provide.

## Real-World Example
A multi-location restaurant group may need a manager. A solo plumber may only need consistent content and GBP updates.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Hiring a person is not the only alternative to doing it yourself.
