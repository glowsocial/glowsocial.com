# What should a dentist post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A dentist should post preventive care tips, patient FAQs, treatment explanations, team introductions, reviews, office updates, and reminders for cleanings or unused benefits.

## Why This Matters
Dental content should reduce anxiety and make the practice feel familiar before a patient books.

## Real-World Example
A dental office can post "what happens during a cleaning," a hygienist introduction, and a review from a happy patient in one week.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Dental social media should not be all promotions. Education and trust are more important than constant discounting.
