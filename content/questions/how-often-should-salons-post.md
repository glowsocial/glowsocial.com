# How often should salons post on social media?

Salons should post 3-5 times per week if they have enough client photos and service content. The minimum useful cadence is 3 posts per week.

## Why This Matters
Salon clients want recent proof. A page with old transformations can make a strong salon look inactive.

## Real-World Example
A salon can post a transformation, stylist tip, review, and booking reminder each week.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Salons do not need to post every appointment. They need a steady rhythm of proof and education.
