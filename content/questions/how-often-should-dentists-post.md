# How often should dentists post on social media?

Most dentists should post 3 times per week. That cadence is enough to keep the practice visible without overwhelming the team.

## Why This Matters
Dental patients need reminders and trust-building content, but daily posting is not necessary for most practices.

## Real-World Example
A weekly mix could include one preventive care tip, one FAQ, and one review or team post.

## What Most People Get Wrong
More posts do not automatically mean more patients. Consistent, useful trust-building content matters more than volume.
