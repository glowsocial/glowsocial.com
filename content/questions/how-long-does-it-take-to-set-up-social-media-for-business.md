# How long does it take to set up social media for a business?

## Direct Answer
15-30 minutes for all major platforms if you use a setup tool that generates your bios and walks you through each step. Doing it manually — writing bios from scratch, figuring out each platform's process, resizing images — takes 3-5 hours.

## Why This Matters
The setup itself isn't hard. What kills most business owners is the decision-making friction. "What should my bio say? What category do I pick? Should I use my personal email or business email? What size should my logo be?" These micro-decisions stack up and turn a 30-minute task into a project that gets pushed to "next week" indefinitely. The businesses that get profiles set up fastest use a tool that eliminates those decisions — scanning their website, generating bios, and providing step-by-step instructions for each platform.

## Real-World Example
A solo chiropractor put "set up social media" on her to-do list for three months. She'd open Instagram, stare at the bio field, not know what to write, and close the app. She finally used the free setup tool at [glowsocial.com/setup](https://glowsocial.com/setup), which scanned her website and generated bios for 8 platforms instantly. ## What Most People Get Wrong
Business owners treat social media setup like a big project requiring a "social media day." It's not. It's a series of 2-3 minute tasks per platform. The bio is the hard part, and if a tool writes it for you, each platform takes less time than ordering coffee. Don't batch it — just do the next one.
