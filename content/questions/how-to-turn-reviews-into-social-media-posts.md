# How do you turn reviews into social media posts?

## Direct Answer
Turn reviews into social media posts by quoting one specific line, adding context about the service, thanking the customer, and connecting the review to a clear trust point.

## Why This Matters
Reviews are proof. Repurposing them keeps trust signals visible beyond the review platform.

## Real-World Example
"Fast same-day repair" can become a post about emergency availability, not just a screenshot of stars.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Do not only post the review image. Explain why the review matters for future customers.
