# What is social media automation?

Social media automation uses software to reduce manual work in planning, creating, scheduling, publishing, or repurposing posts. Some tools only schedule content; others also generate posts.

## Why This Matters
Automation can solve different problems. A business should know whether it needs publishing automation, content creation, or both.

## Real-World Example
Buffer automates scheduling. Glow Social automates more of the content creation and posting workflow for local businesses.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Automation does not automatically mean generic content. Good automation uses business-specific context.
