# What is the best cheap social media management option?

## Direct Answer
The best cheap social media management option is the one that solves your actual bottleneck. If you already have content, a scheduler may be cheapest. If you do not know what to post, a done-for-you service is usually cheaper than losing hours every week to DIY content creation or hiring a freelancer.

## The Real Test
Ask: "What part of social media keeps failing?"

If the answer is "we forget to publish," buy scheduling. If the answer is "we never know what to say," scheduling will not solve it. You need content creation.

## When The Cheapest Option Is Not Actually Cheap
DIY looks free until you count the time. If a business owner spends four hours per week planning, writing, designing, and publishing posts, that is 16 hours per month. Even at $50/hour, the hidden cost is $800/month.

## What Most People Get Wrong
They shop for software when they need an outcome. A cheap tool that creates another task is not cheap if nobody uses it.
