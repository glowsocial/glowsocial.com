# What is the best cheap social media management option?

## Direct Answer
The best cheap social media management option depends on the bottleneck. Use a free scheduler if you already create content. Use done-for-you automation if content creation is the problem. Hire a freelancer only if you need custom attention and have the budget.

## Why This Matters
Cheap software can become expensive if it still requires hours of owner time every month.

## Real-World Example
A $15 scheduler plus 8 hours of owner time can cost more than a $99 done-for-you plan.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The lowest subscription price is not always the lowest total cost.
