# Do social media posts help local SEO?

## Direct Answer
Social media posts can indirectly help local SEO by keeping profiles active, reinforcing business information, driving branded searches, supporting Google Business Profile activity, and giving customers more trust signals before they call.

## Why This Matters
Social posts are not a direct replacement for reviews, website pages, or citations. But they support the public footprint customers and search systems evaluate.

## Real-World Example
A landscaping company posting current project photos and GBP updates looks more active when customers compare it in search.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Social media does not magically rank a website by itself. It supports trust, freshness, and discovery.
