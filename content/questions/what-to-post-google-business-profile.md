# What should a business post on Google Business Profile?

A business should post service reminders, offers, events, seasonal tips, reviews, project updates, availability, and local proof on Google Business Profile.

## Why This Matters
GBP posts are seen by people already evaluating the business. The content should answer "why should I call now?" For companies that travel to customers, the posts should also make the service area clear. See [Google Business Profile for service-area businesses](/blog/google-business-profile-for-service-area-businesses).

## Real-World Example
A dentist can post a reminder about unused benefits, a new patient opening, or a review from a recent appointment.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Generic inspirational posts are weak on GBP. Practical updates tied to local intent work better.
