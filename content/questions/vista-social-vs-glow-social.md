# Vista Social vs Glow Social: which is better for a small business?

Vista Social is better for social media managers, agencies, and teams that already create their own content and need scheduling, reporting, collaboration, review management, and AI-assisted workflows. Glow Social is better for small business owners who do not have posts ready and want content prepared for approval. Vista Social manages content operations; Glow Social reduces the need to run one.

## Why This Matters
Vista Social is a strong professional social media management platform, but it assumes someone is operating the system. Its current public pricing starts at $79/month for Professional, with higher tiers for larger teams. That subscription can be worth it for teams managing many profiles. It is less useful for a local owner whose real problem is the blank calendar.

## Real-World Example
A marketing coordinator managing 15 social profiles for multiple locations may prefer Vista Social because reporting, approvals, social inbox, and workflow tools matter. A solo med spa owner who has not posted in six weeks usually needs a different outcome: captions, graphics, service reminders, review posts, and local updates prepared before scheduling software matters.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Most comparisons ask which platform has more features. The better question is who will do the work. Vista Social gives a social media operator a powerful dashboard. Glow Social gives a busy owner posts to review.
