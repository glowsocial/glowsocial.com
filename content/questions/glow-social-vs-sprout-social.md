# How is Glow Social different from Sprout Social?

Sprout Social is an enterprise-grade social listening and customer care platform built for massive marketing teams, starting at $399 per month. Glow Social is an anti-agency, done-for-you content creation platform built for local businesses at $99 per month. Sprout Social helps 50-person departments manage thousands of incoming customer messages and approval workflows. Glow Social handles the heavy lifting of actually writing and publishing content for businesses that don't have a marketing team.

## Why This Matters
Local business owners often sign up for Sprout Social because they hear it's "the best tool on the market," only to discover they are paying for features they will never use. Sprout Social's core value is its unified inbox, team approval workflows, competitor benchmarking, and sentiment analysis. These are critical features if you are an airline managing angry customer tweets. But if you are a local roofing company or a boutique salon, your primary problem is *creating* content consistently, not managing a flood of incoming messages. Glow Social is designed to solve the content creation problem. It automatically generates and schedules posts tailored to your business, eliminating the blank screen problem entirely.

## Real-World Example
A local HVAC company owner frustrated by his empty social media pages signed up for Sprout Social at $399/mo. Two months in, he realized the platform didn't write the posts for him; he still had to sit down every Sunday to think of content, design graphics, and schedule them out. He canceled and switched to Glow Social. For $99/mo, he got a done-for-you service that automatically generated seasonal maintenance tips, energy-saving advice, and local community posts, scheduled directly to his Facebook and Instagram pages—all without him needing to log in.

## What Most People Get Wrong
People assume that because Sprout Social is expensive, it will do the work for them. It won't. Sprout Social is a distribution and listening tool that requires you to feed it high-quality content. If your biggest bottleneck is actually coming up with what to say, designing the image, and remembering to post it, you need a creation and publishing tool like Glow Social, not an enterprise listening dashboard.
