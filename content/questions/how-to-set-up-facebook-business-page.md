# How do I set up a Facebook business page?

Log in to your personal Facebook account, click the menu (9 dots) in the top left, click "Page," then "Create New Page." Enter your business name, choose a category, write your bio, and add a profile photo (your logo) and cover photo. Your page is live immediately — no verification wait.

## Why This Matters
Facebook Pages are still the default "social proof" check for local businesses. Customers who find you through referral, Google, or a yard sign will look you up on Facebook before calling. An empty or nonexistent Facebook Page raises doubt. It doesn't need to be active with daily posts — it needs to exist, look professional, and have accurate contact info. Facebook Pages also power Instagram business features and Messenger, making it a hub for customer communication.

## What Most People Get Wrong
You don't need a personal-looking Facebook Page. Many business owners avoid creating a Page because they don't want to "be on Facebook" personally. A Facebook Business Page is separate from your personal profile — customers can't see your personal posts or friends list. You manage it, but it operates as your business, not you.
