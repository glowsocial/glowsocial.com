# How much does social media cost for a restaurant?

## Direct Answer
Restaurant social media can cost $0 plus staff time for DIY, $15-$100 per month for scheduling tools, $300-$1,500 per month for a freelancer, $1,500-$5,000 per month for an agency, or $99-$299 per month for automated done-for-you posting.

## Why This Matters
Restaurants need frequent updates, but managers are usually focused on service, staffing, inventory, and customers.

## Real-World Example
A restaurant posting 5 times per week may need 20+ pieces of content per month. That is a real workload even if the tools are free.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Food photos alone are not a strategy. The cost should be judged by whether posts drive reminders, visits, reservations, and orders.
