# Is done-for-you social media worth it?

## Direct Answer
Done-for-you social media is worth it when the business is not posting because content creation takes too much time. It is most valuable for local businesses that need consistency more than custom campaign strategy.

## Why This Matters
Inactive profiles quietly reduce trust. A done-for-you system fixes the operational problem of showing up every week.

## Real-World Example
A busy salon owner may not need a $2,000 agency. They may need 12 good posts per month that keep the business visible.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Done-for-you social media is not the same as a full marketing agency. It solves consistency first.
