# How do I set up social media profiles for my business?

Start with Google Business Profile, then create accounts on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and any platform your customers use. Use a free setup tool like [glowsocial.com/setup](https://glowsocial.com/setup) — it scans your website, writes your bios for 8 platforms, and walks you through each one step by step.

## Why This Matters
Most small businesses stall during setup because each platform has a different process, different character limits, and different fields. Writing 8 bios from scratch takes 2-3 hours, and half of them end up inconsistent or left blank. A tool that auto-generates bios from your website content gives you consistent messaging across every platform in minutes. Your business name, services, and location stay aligned everywhere customers look for you.

## Real-World Example
A solo massage therapist with a website and zero social media presence used a free setup tool to scan her site. It pulled her business name, services, and location, then generated bios for Google Business Profile, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, X, Bluesky, and Threads — all with correct character counts. She copied and pasted each one while following the step-by-step instructions. Total time: 22 minutes for all 8 platforms.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Business owners think they need a strategy before creating profiles. They wait weeks "planning content" before ever making an account. The profile itself IS the strategy — a complete profile with a clear bio, your services, and your location makes you findable immediately. You don't need a single post to start showing up in search results on most platforms.
