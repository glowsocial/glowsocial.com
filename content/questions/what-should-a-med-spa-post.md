# What should a med spa post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A med spa should post treatment education, provider credibility, before-and-after results with permission, aftercare tips, reviews, FAQs, and booking reminders.

## Why This Matters
Med spa clients care about results and safety. Content should make the provider feel both skilled and trustworthy.

## Real-World Example
A Botox or facial treatment can become a "who it is for" post, an aftercare tip, a provider explanation, and a review post.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Med spas should not rely only on aesthetic visuals. Education and provider authority are what convert cautious clients.
