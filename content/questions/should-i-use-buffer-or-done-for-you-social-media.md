# Should I use Buffer or done-for-you social media?

Use Buffer if you already create your own content and mainly need scheduling. Use done-for-you social media if the hard part is deciding what to post, writing captions, creating graphics, and staying consistent.

## Why This Matters
Scheduling and content creation are different problems. Many businesses buy scheduling software when their real bottleneck is the blank screen.

## Real-World Example
A restaurant with daily food photos may do well with Buffer. A plumber with no time to write posts may need done-for-you content instead.

## What Most People Get Wrong
A cheaper scheduler is not cheaper if it still requires hours of work every month.
