# How many social media posts per month does a business need?

## Direct Answer
Most local businesses need at least 12 social media posts per month, or about 3 per week. Restaurants, retail shops, salons, and event-driven businesses may need 16-30 posts per month.

## Why This Matters
Posting volume should match buying behavior, available content, and the need to look active.

## Real-World Example
A dentist may do well with 12 posts per month, while a restaurant with specials may need 20+.

## What Most People Get Wrong
More posts only help if they are useful. Consistency beats random bursts.
