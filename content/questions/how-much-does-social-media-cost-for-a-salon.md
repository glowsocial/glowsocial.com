# How much does social media cost for a salon?

## Direct Answer
Salon social media usually costs $0 plus owner time for DIY, $300-$1,500 per month for a freelancer, $1,500+ per month for an agency, or $99-$299 per month for done-for-you automation.

## Why This Matters
Salons need consistent visual proof, but owners and stylists are often booked behind the chair.

## Real-World Example
A salon can spend hours each month turning transformation photos into captions, reels, and booking posts. Automation reduces the blank-screen work.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The hard part is not taking one good photo. The hard part is turning photos, reviews, and service education into a steady calendar.
