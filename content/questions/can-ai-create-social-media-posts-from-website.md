# Can AI create social media posts from a website?

## Direct Answer
Yes, AI can create social media posts from a website by using the site's services, tone, location, offers, and customer language as source material. This usually produces better content than a generic prompt.

## Why This Matters
The website already contains the facts a social post needs: what the business does, who it serves, and how it describes value.

## Real-World Example
A landscaper's service page can become posts about xeriscape, irrigation checks, before-and-after photos, and seasonal maintenance.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The website scan is only the start. Good posts still need a useful mix of education, proof, and calls-to-action.
