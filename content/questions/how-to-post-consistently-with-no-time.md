# How do you post consistently when you have no time?

## Direct Answer
Post consistently with no time by reducing the decision load: use recurring categories, batch ideas once per month, repurpose reviews and FAQs, and automate publishing or use a done-for-you service.

## Why This Matters
Most business owners stop posting because each post starts from scratch.

## Real-World Example
One customer FAQ can become a Facebook post, Instagram caption, GBP update, and LinkedIn post with small edits.

## What Most People Get Wrong
The solution is not more motivation. It is a system that removes daily decisions.
