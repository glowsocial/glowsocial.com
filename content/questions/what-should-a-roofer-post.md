# What should a roofer post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A roofer should post project photos, before-and-after work, storm damage education, inspection reminders, customer reviews, material explanations, and service-area updates.

## Why This Matters
Roofing customers are making expensive decisions. Public proof reduces risk before the sales conversation.

## Real-World Example
A storm-season post can explain when to schedule an inspection and show a recent repair from the same area.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Roofing content should not only chase leads. It should build trust long before a homeowner needs a quote.
