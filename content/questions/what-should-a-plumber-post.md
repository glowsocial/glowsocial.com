# What should a plumber post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A plumber should post maintenance tips, emergency prevention advice, customer reviews, before-and-after job photos, water heater reminders, drain care tips, and service-area updates.

## Why This Matters
Plumbing content builds trust before an urgent call. The customer may not need help today, but they remember the helpful company when a problem appears.

## Real-World Example
One week could include a shutoff-valve tip, a review, and a reminder to flush water heaters annually.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Plumbing posts do not need to be funny or viral. They need to make the company look competent, responsive, and local.
