# How often should plumbers post on social media?

Plumbers should post at least 3 times per week: one maintenance tip, one proof post such as a review or job photo, and one service reminder or Google Business Profile update.

## Why This Matters
Plumbing demand is intermittent. Consistent posting keeps the company familiar before an urgent need appears.

## Real-World Example
Monday can be a prevention tip, Wednesday a review, and Friday a seasonal service reminder.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Posting only during slow weeks is backwards. Visibility works best before customers need you.
