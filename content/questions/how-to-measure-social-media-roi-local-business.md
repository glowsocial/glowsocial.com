# How do you measure social media ROI for a local business?

## Direct Answer
Measure social media ROI for a local business by tracking influenced leads, calls, bookings, website clicks, Google Business Profile actions, customer mentions, and the profit from customers who saw or checked the business online.

## Why This Matters
Local social media often influences trust before the final search or phone call. Not every conversion shows up as a direct click.

## Real-World Example
Ask new customers, "Had you seen us online before?" and record the answer alongside calls and bookings.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Likes are not ROI. Revenue, calls, appointments, and customer confidence matter more.
