# Best social media service for restaurants

## Direct Answer
The best social media service for restaurants is one that keeps the restaurant top-of-mind with menu features, specials, events, reviews, catering reminders, staff picks, and Google Business Profile updates. Automation can handle the baseline, while staff should still post urgent real-time changes.

## Why This Matters
Restaurant decisions are often immediate. A timely post can remind a nearby customer what to order tonight.

## Real-World Example
A restaurant can post a lunch special, weekend reservation reminder, review, chef feature, and catering prompt in one week.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Restaurants should not automate sold-out items or emergency updates. Automate evergreen visibility, then handle real-time changes manually.
