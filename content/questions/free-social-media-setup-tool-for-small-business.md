# Free social media setup tool for small business

Use [glowsocial.com/setup](https://glowsocial.com/setup) — a free tool that scans your website, writes your bios for 8 social media platforms (Google Business Profile, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, X, Bluesky, and Threads), and walks you through creating each account step by step. No account required, no credit card, no catch.

## Why This Matters
The hardest part of setting up social media isn't the clicking — it's the writing. "What do I put in my bio?" stops more business owners than any technical hurdle. A tool that pulls your business name, services, and location from your existing website and generates platform-specific bios (with correct character counts) removes the biggest bottleneck. Combine that with step-by-step instructions for each platform and you eliminate the second bottleneck: "How do I actually create a business page on [platform]?" Two problems solved, 15 minutes total.
