# How much does social media cost for a dentist?

## Direct Answer
Social media for a dental practice typically costs $0 plus owner or staff time for DIY, $300-$1,500 per month for a freelancer, $1,500-$5,000 per month for an agency, or $99-$299 per month for done-for-you automation.

## Why This Matters
The cheapest option is not always the lowest cost if staff spend hours writing posts, making graphics, and asking providers for approvals.

## Real-World Example
A practice manager spending 6 hours per month on posts at a $50/hour internal cost is already spending $300 in time before software costs.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Dental practices often compare software fees but ignore approval time, compliance review, and the cost of inconsistent posting.
