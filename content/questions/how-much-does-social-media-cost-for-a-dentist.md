# How much does social media cost for a dentist?

## Direct Answer
Social media for a dental practice usually costs $0 plus staff time for DIY, $15-$100/month for scheduling software, $300-$1,500/month for a freelancer, $1,500+/month for an agency, or $99-$299/month for done-for-you automation. The right price depends on whether the practice needs consistency, custom creative, patient education, or full marketing strategy.

## What Dental Practices Should Pay For
Pay for content that reduces patient uncertainty: treatment explainers, oral health tips, team introductions, reviews, appointment reminders, and FAQs.

## The Break-Even Question
If one new patient is worth hundreds or thousands in lifetime value, a consistent posting system does not need to influence many bookings to be worthwhile.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Dental practices often compare tools by price instead of asking who will actually create the patient-friendly content every week.
