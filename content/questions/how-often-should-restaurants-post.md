# How often should restaurants post on social media?

## Direct Answer
Restaurants should usually post 4-7 times per week, depending on specials, events, menu changes, and available photos. Daily posting can make sense for restaurants with frequent updates.

## Why This Matters
Restaurant posts are timely. Customers make decisions about lunch, dinner, reservations, and takeout quickly.

## Real-World Example
A restaurant can post a weekly special, menu feature, review, weekend reminder, and event post.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Restaurants should automate the baseline but still handle urgent real-time updates manually.
