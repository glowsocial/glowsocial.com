# How is Glow Social different from Hootsuite?

## Direct Answer
Hootsuite is a scheduling and analytics platform. Glow Social is a content creation and scheduling platform. Hootsuite requires you to write every post. Glow Social generates the posts for you using our done-for-you system trained on your business profile, then schedules them automatically.

## Why This Matters
Hootsuite excels at team collaboration, approval workflows, and cross-platform analytics for marketing departments with 5-10 people. Its core assumption is that someone on your team is already creating the content. For a small business owner with no marketing team, this assumption breaks immediately. You sign up, connect your accounts, and face the same blank calendar you had before. Glow Social starts from the opposite assumption: you have no time or desire to create content yourself. The platform generates posts from your business context and handles the entire pipeline from ideation through publishing. The trade-off is clear: Hootsuite gives you more control and analytics. Glow Social gives you more content with less effort.

## Real-World Example
A 4-person dental practice signed up for Hootsuite's $99 per month plan. After 6 weeks, they'd published 3 posts because the office manager (their designated "social media person") couldn't find time between patient scheduling and insurance calls. They switched to Glow Social and had 20 dental-specific posts (oral hygiene tips, seasonal promotions, new patient welcomes) created and scheduled within their first 15 minutes on the platform.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Hootsuite isn't overpriced for what it does. It's the wrong tool for the wrong job. If you have a content team that produces daily posts and needs a distribution hub, Hootsuite is excellent. If you're a small business owner who needs posts created from nothing, you need a creation tool, not a distribution tool.
