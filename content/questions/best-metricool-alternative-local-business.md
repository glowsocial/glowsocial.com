# What is the best Metricool alternative for a local business?

## Direct Answer
The best Metricool alternative depends on the job. Use Buffer for simpler scheduling, Later or Plann for visual planning, Hootsuite or Vista Social for broader management workflows, and Glow Social if the business needs posts prepared for approval. Metricool is strongest when analytics, reporting, competitor tracking, and multi-brand scheduling matter.

## Why This Matters
Metricool has a real free plan, but its free tier is limited: one brand, 20 scheduled posts per month, 30 days of analytics, and no LinkedIn or X/Twitter connection. Paid plans add more brands, unlimited publishing, LinkedIn connection, reporting, and deeper analytics. That makes Metricool useful for creators, agencies, and analytical teams. It does not remove the need to decide what to post.

## Real-World Example
A freelance social media manager handling several clients may prefer Metricool because reporting and competitor analysis help justify the work. A solo roofer trying to keep Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Google Business Profile active usually needs job-photo posts, review highlights, storm-prep tips, and service reminders prepared first.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Most Metricool alternative lists compare analytics features. For local businesses, the first filter should be whether the tool creates useful posts or only measures posts the business still has to make.
