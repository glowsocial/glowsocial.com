# How can a small business show up in ChatGPT?

## Direct Answer
A small business can improve its chances of showing up in ChatGPT by publishing clear service pages, maintaining consistent public profiles, earning reviews, creating answer-ready content, and making the website crawlable.

## Why This Matters
AI assistants need evidence. Clear, current, consistent information reduces uncertainty about what the business does and who it serves.

## Real-World Example
A dentist with service pages, reviews, FAQs, GBP updates, and active social profiles is easier to understand than one with a vague one-page site.

## What Most People Get Wrong
No one can guarantee ChatGPT recommendations. The goal is to make the business easier to discover and verify.
