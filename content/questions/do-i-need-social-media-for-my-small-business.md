# Do I need social media for my small business?

Yes — but not for the reason most people think. You don't need social media to "go viral." You need it because it's where people verify you exist. When someone hears your name from a friend, gets your business card, or finds you on Google, their next step is checking your social media profiles. No profiles = no trust.

## Why This Matters
Social media profiles function as digital storefronts for local businesses. A 2024 survey by BrightLocal found that 87% of consumers used the internet to evaluate local businesses. When potential customers can't find you on the platform they use most, they don't assume you're just "not on social media" — they assume you're not established, not active, or not trustworthy. Even a profile with your logo, bio, hours, and a link to your website (and zero posts) performs better than no profile at all. The profiles themselves are findable in Google search results, which multiplies your visibility.
