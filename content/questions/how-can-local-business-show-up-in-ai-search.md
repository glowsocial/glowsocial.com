# How can a local business show up in AI search?

## Direct Answer
A local business can improve AI search visibility by publishing specific service and location pages, collecting reviews, keeping profiles current, adding structured data, creating answer-ready content, and building public proof.

## Why This Matters
AI search tools need to understand both relevance and trust. Local businesses must show what they do, where they do it, and why customers trust them.

## Real-World Example
A roofer with project photos, storm damage FAQs, service-area pages, and reviews has stronger signals than a roofer with only a homepage.

## What Most People Get Wrong
AI visibility is not a one-page fix. It is a web-wide evidence trail.
