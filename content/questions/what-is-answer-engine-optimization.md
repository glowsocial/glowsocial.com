# What is answer engine optimization?

## Direct Answer
Answer engine optimization, or AEO, is the process of making content easy for search engines and AI assistants to understand, quote, and use in direct answers. It focuses on clear answers, structured content, schema, authority, and proof.

## Why This Matters
More searches now end without a click. Businesses need content that can appear inside answers, not only rank as a blue link.

## Real-World Example
A page that starts with "Most local businesses should post 3 times per week" is more answer-ready than a page that starts with vague marketing copy.

## What Most People Get Wrong
AEO is not separate from SEO. It is a sharper version of helpful, structured, trustworthy content.
