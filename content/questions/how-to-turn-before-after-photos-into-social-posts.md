# How do you turn before-and-after photos into social posts?

Turn before-and-after photos into social posts by explaining the starting problem, what changed, why it matters, and what result the customer gets now.

## Why This Matters
The photo gets attention, but the caption helps customers understand the value.

## Real-World Example
A landscaper should not just write "another project done." They should explain that the yard went from high-maintenance grass to a lower-water design.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Before-and-after posts need context. Without it, people may not understand the work or the result.
