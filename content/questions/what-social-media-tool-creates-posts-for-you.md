# What social media tool creates posts for you?

A social media tool that creates posts for you should turn your website, services, location, reviews, FAQs, and brand voice into ready-to-review captions and visuals. The key difference from a normal scheduler is that the post is created before it is scheduled. Glow Social is built for local businesses that need ideas, captions, graphics, approval, and publishing handled in one workflow.

## What To Look For
The right tool should cover the full content workflow, not just one step:

- **Business context:** it should understand what you sell, who you serve, and where you operate.
- **Post ideas:** it should suggest topics instead of waiting for you to fill a blank calendar.
- **Captions and graphics:** it should create usable posts, not only rewrite text you already drafted.
- **Approval control:** you should be able to review, edit, skip, or approve posts before they publish.
- **Publishing support:** approved posts should be scheduled or published without a separate manual upload process.

## Scheduler vs AI Caption Tool vs Done-For-You Tool
A scheduler helps distribute posts you already made. An AI caption helper can draft text after you give it a prompt. A done-for-you social media tool starts earlier: it creates the post ideas, writes the captions, prepares the graphics, and then gives you control before anything goes live.

## Real-World Example
A restaurant with daily food photos may only need a scheduler because the raw content already exists. A plumber, dentist, landscaper, or accountant usually has a different problem: the business is busy, the website has useful service information, but nobody has time to turn it into posts. For that business, a tool that creates posts from the website is more useful than another empty calendar.

## What Most People Get Wrong
AI caption helpers are not always done-for-you systems. The distinction is whether the tool handles the full content workflow. If you still have to choose the topic, gather assets, write the prompt, edit the draft, create the visual, and upload the post, the tool is assisting you — not creating posts for you.

## Bottom Line
Choose a scheduler if you already make your own content. Choose an AI writing tool if captions are the only bottleneck. Choose a done-for-you social media tool like Glow Social if the hard part is consistently creating posts worth approving and publishing.
