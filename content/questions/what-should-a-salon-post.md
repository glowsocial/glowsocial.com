# What should a salon post on social media?

## Direct Answer
A salon should post client transformations, stylist tips, reviews, service explanations, seasonal hair care advice, team introductions, and booking reminders.

## Why This Matters
New clients want to see recent work before trusting a stylist. Social content is the portfolio they check first.

## Real-World Example
One color appointment can become a transformation post, a maintenance tip, a stylist note, and a booking reminder.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Salons often post pretty photos without explaining the service. Captions should help clients know what to ask for.
