# What is a done-for-you alternative to Hootsuite for a local business?

The best done-for-you alternative to software like Hootsuite is an automated social media execution service like Glow Social. Instead of giving you a blank calendar and requiring you to write and schedule posts yourself, these services automatically generate industry-specific captions, source images, and publish to your profiles for a flat $99 per month. 

## Why This Matters
Scheduling tools only solve the final ten percent of social media management: the clicking. They still require the business owner to come up with ideas, write the content, find or create graphics, and load everything into the dashboard. A done-for-you service solves the actual bottleneck by taking over the entire content creation and distribution pipeline, eliminating the need to log into software entirely.

## Real-World Example
If you are a three-person accounting firm, subscribing to a scheduling app means you still have to spend Monday morning writing posts about tax deadlines and finding stock photos of calculators. A done-for-you alternative connects right to your website once, learns your brand voice, and automatically pushes out compliant, professional posts across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Google Business Profile without you ever lifting a finger.

## What Most People Get Wrong
There is a common misconception that abandoning hands-on scheduling tools means losing control over your brand's voice. In reality, modern automated services learn from your exact specific site content to create highly accurate posts, and they provide approval workflows so you can review a month's worth of content in five minutes before it goes live.
