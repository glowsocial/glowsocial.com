2.  **No Strategy:** They post just to "check the box," without a mix of educational and promotional content.
3.  **Human Error:** Low-cost freelancers often juggle 20+ clients, leading to typos and missed posts.

## 4 Keys to Finding the Best Affordable Service

To ensure you're getting value and not just a low price, look for these four elements:

### 1. Multi-Platform Support
An affordable service should sync your content across LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok without extra fees.
*Source: [Pew Research on Social Media Usage](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/fact-sheet/social-media/)*

### 2. Strategic Content Mix
Ensure the service doesn't just "sell." It should follow a 60/30/10 rule: 60% educational, 30% brand-building, and 10% promotional.

### 3. Ease of Setup
If it takes 10 hours to onboard, it's not truly affordable when you factor in the value of your own time. Look for "5-minute setup" solutions.

### 4. No Long-Term Contracts
True affordability comes with flexibility. Avoid agencies that lock you into 6-month or 12-month commitments.
