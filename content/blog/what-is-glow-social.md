Glow Social is an AI-powered social media management platform that generates content and posts it to 12 platforms automatically. It was built specifically for the people who know they need to be on social media — but do not have the time or bandwidth to do it manually.

## What Glow Social actually does

Most social media tools are scheduling platforms: you create the content, upload it, and the tool distributes it on a timer. Glow Social is different. It handles both sides of the equation — content generation and distribution — in one system.

Here is what happens inside a typical Glow Social account:

1. **Onboarding captures your business context** — industry, brand voice, content goals, and topics to emphasize or avoid
2. **AI generates a full content calendar** — platform-appropriate captions for each connected channel, based on your business context
3. **Content is reviewed (optional)** — you can approve posts before they go live, or let them post automatically
4. **Analytics track performance** — engagement, reach, and follower growth across all connected platforms in one dashboard

This replaces what used to require three separate tools: an AI writing tool for captions, a scheduling platform for distribution, and an analytics dashboard to measure results.

## The 12 platforms Glow Social connects to

Glow Social supports posting across:

- **Facebook** (Pages and Groups)
- **Instagram** (feed posts, Reels, Stories)
- **LinkedIn** (personal profiles and Company Pages)
- **TikTok**
- **YouTube** (shorts and standard uploads)
- **Pinterest**
- **X / Twitter**
- **Google Business Profile**
- **Threads**
- **Bluesky**

For agency owners who manage multiple client accounts, each client gets their own workspace with separate platform connections, brand voice settings, and content calendars.

## Who uses Glow Social

### Social media agency owners

Agency owners use Glow Social to run multiple client accounts at scale. Instead of spending 30–60 minutes per client creating content manually, the AI handles the first draft for every client automatically. Most agency owners using Glow Social report spending 10–15 minutes per client per week on review and approval.

### Solopreneurs and coaches

Experts who have built a following or reputation — consultants, coaches, course creators, authors — use Glow Social to stay visible on social media without making it a second job. The platform keeps their profiles active consistently, which is the single most important factor in social media growth.

### Local service businesses

Local businesses that understand the value of social media presence but do not have staff to manage it — restaurants, gyms, salons, medical practices, contractors — use Glow Social to maintain a professional presence across multiple platforms without hiring a social media manager.

## The problem Glow Social solves

The most common social media problem for small business owners and solo agency owners is not platform access or analytics — it is consistency. The businesses that win on social media post regularly, week after week, without long gaps.

The barrier to consistency is almost always time and creative energy, not intent. Most business owners know they should post more. They lack the system to do it.

Glow Social is built around that specific constraint. The AI handles the creative output. The owner handles the business. Social media runs in the background.
