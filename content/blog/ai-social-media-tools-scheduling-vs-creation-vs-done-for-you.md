Every social media tool now claims to be "AI-powered." Buffer has AI. Hootsuite has AI. Vista Social has AI. Jasper, Copy.ai, SocialBee — all AI.

But these tools use AI in fundamentally different ways. Understanding the difference is critical to choosing the right tool.

<tr><th>Category</th><th>What AI Does</th><th>What You Do</th><th>Time Saved</th><th>Examples</th></tr>

## Category 1: AI-Enhanced Scheduling Tools

**What they are:** Traditional scheduling platforms (you create, they schedule) with AI features bolted on.

**How AI helps:**
<ul>
<li>AI suggests captions based on your topic or image</li>
<li>AI recommends optimal posting times</li>
<li>AI generates reply suggestions for comments/DMs</li>
<li>AI summarizes analytics into plain-English insights</li>
</ul>

**What you still do:**
<ul>
<li>Come up with content ideas</li>
<li>Create or source images</li>
<li>Review and edit AI-generated captions</li>
<li>Fill your content calendar</li>
<li>Monitor and respond to engagement</li>
</ul>

**Time investment:** 5-8 hours/month (down from 8-12 without AI)

**Examples and pricing:**
<table>
<tr><th>Tool</th><th>Starting Price</th><th>AI Features</th></tr>
<tr><td>Buffer</td><td>Free-$15/mo</td><td>AI caption generation</td></tr>
<tr><td>Vista Social</td><td>$39-199/mo</td><td>AI assistant, smart scheduling, AI training</td></tr>
<tr><td>SocialBee</td><td>$29-99/mo</td><td>AI caption writing, category rotation</td></tr>
<tr><td>Hootsuite</td><td>$99-249/mo</td><td>AI content creation, best time to post</td></tr>

**Best for:** Social media managers and teams who already create content and want to work faster.

## Category 2: AI Content Generation Tools

**What they are:** AI writing tools that generate social media text you then publish elsewhere.

**How AI helps:**
<ul>
<li>Generates caption variations from a prompt</li>
<li>Converts blog posts into social media posts</li>
<li>Writes in different tones and styles</li>
<li>Creates hashtag suggestions</li>
</ul>

**What you still do:**
<ul>
<li>Provide prompts or source material</li>
<li>Edit and customize AI output</li>
<li>Create or source visual content</li>
<li>Copy text into a scheduling tool</li>
<li>Schedule and publish manually</li>
</ul>

**Time investment:** 4-6 hours/month (down from 8-12 without AI)

**Examples and pricing:**
<table>
<tr><th>Tool</th><th>Starting Price</th><th>Specialty</th></tr>
<tr><td>Jasper</td><td>$99/mo</td><td>Marketing copy, brand voice consistency</td></tr>

<tr><td>Lately</td><td>Custom</td><td>Long-form to social repurposing</td></tr>
<tr><td>ChatGPT</td><td>$0-20/mo</td><td>General purpose, unlimited output</td></tr>
</table>

**Best for:** Content marketers who create long-form content and want help repurposing it for social.
