Copy.ai and Glow Social both use AI for social media content, but they're completely different tools. <strong>Copy.ai</strong> is a writing assistant—you prompt it, it generates text, you edit and schedule everything yourself. <strong>Glow Social</strong> is done-for-you posting—it creates and publishes 12 posts per month for $49 without you doing anything.

The question isn't about content quality. It's about how much work you want to do.

## What Copy.ai Does Well

<ul>
<li>Free tier for basic use</li>
<li>Generates text for any format</li>
<li>Multiple templates and workflows</li>
<li>Good for teams doing high-volume content</li>
</ul>

<strong>Best for:</strong> Marketers who need AI writing across multiple channels.

<strong>The catch:</strong> Copy.ai writes words. You still need Canva for graphics, Buffer for scheduling, and your time to manage everything.

## What Glow Social Does Well

<li>Customized to your business type</li>
<li>One tool replaces three</li>
</ul>

<strong>Best for:</strong> Business owners who want social media handled completely.

<strong>The catch:</strong> Social media only. Copy.ai works for blogs, emails, ads, and more.
