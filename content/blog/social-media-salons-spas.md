## DIY vs Automated: Salon Reality Check

<strong>DIY works if:</strong>

<ul>
<li>You have time between clients for content creation</li>
<li>You consistently remember to take photos of your best work</li>
<li>You won't stop posting when you're fully booked</li>
<li>You have a team member who handles marketing</li>
</ul>

<strong>Automated posting works if:</strong>

<ul>
<li>You're booked back-to-back with no marketing time</li>
<li>You've tried DIY but posting always drops off</li>
<li>You'd rather focus on clients than content calendars</li>
<li>Slow seasons hit because you didn't post during busy seasons</li>
</ul>
