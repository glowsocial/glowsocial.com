# I don't have time for social media

## Direct Answer
You're not wrong — managing social media properly takes 8 to 12 hours per month when done manually. But you don't have to do it manually anymore. AI-powered done-for-you platforms create and publish professional posts automatically, reducing your time commitment from hours per week to minutes per month.

## Why This Matters
"I don't have time" is the most common reason small business owners give for [inconsistent posting](/blog/why-is-posting-consistently-on-social-media-so-hard). And it's completely valid. Between serving customers, managing employees, handling bookkeeping, and actually doing the work your business is built on, social media is never the most urgent task. The problem is that skipping it has compounding consequences — algorithms deprioritize inactive accounts, customers start checking your competitors instead, and [restarting after a gap](/blog/how-to-restart-social-media-after-long-break) feels harder than starting from scratch.

## Real-World Example
A plumber in Mesa told us he knew social media mattered but couldn't justify spending his Saturday afternoons writing Instagram captions when he had invoices to send and a family to see. He tried batching content once a month but quit after two months because even that felt like too much. When he switched to an automated platform that generates and publishes posts from his business information, his total monthly time investment dropped to five minutes — just enough to glance at the month's content and hit "approve."

## What Most People Get Wrong
Assuming the only two options are "do it yourself" or "hire someone expensive." A freelance social media manager costs $300-800/month. An agency costs $1,500+. But [AI done-for-you tools](/blog/whats-the-best-way-to-automate-social-media-posting) can handle the entire workflow — content creation, image generation, scheduling, and publishing — for under $100/month with virtually zero time investment on your end.
