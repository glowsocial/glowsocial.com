## Direct Answer

Glow Social generates and schedules social media posts automatically. You connect your social accounts, answer a few questions about your business, and the platform creates personalized posts with captions and images, then publishes them on your chosen schedule across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

## Why This Matters

Most social media tools solve half the problem. Scheduling tools like Buffer and Later handle distribution but require you to create every post. Design tools like Canva help with visuals but don't write captions or decide what topics to cover. Glow Social combines content strategy, copywriting, image generation, and scheduling into a single platform. The system uses your website, industry, and business profile to generate posts that match your specific services and voice, not generic templates. Each post is editable before it goes live, so you maintain full control without starting from a blank screen.

## Real-World Example

A 2-location yoga studio connects their Instagram and Facebook accounts, enters their business type and website URL, and Glow Social generates 12 posts covering class schedules, wellness tips, instructor spotlights, and seasonal promotions. The posts are scheduled across both platforms for the month. The studio owner reviews and tweaks any posts she wants to adjust, approves the rest, and her social media runs on autopilot. Total time: under 15 minutes per month.

## What Most People Get Wrong

"Automated social media" usually triggers concerns about robotic, impersonal content. Glow Social pulls context from your actual business (your services, location, industry language) rather than generating from generic prompts. The result sounds like a knowledgeable assistant who studied your website, not a chatbot recycling marketing templates.
