## Direct Answer

Plumbers should use Google Business Profile posts for practical, high-intent updates: emergency availability, service reminders, water heater maintenance, drain tips, customer reviews, service-area posts, and seasonal warnings.

## GBP Post Examples for Plumbers

### Emergency availability

No hot water or a sudden leak? We have service appointments available this week. Call to get on the schedule.

### Drain maintenance

Slow drains are often an early warning sign. If more than one drain is slow, schedule service before it turns into a backup.

### Water heater reminder

Annual water heater flushing can help reduce sediment buildup and extend system life.

### Seasonal post

Before leaving town, make sure everyone in the house knows where the main water shutoff valve is.

### Review highlight

Thank you to our local customer for trusting us with a same-day repair.

## Cadence

Post 1-2 times per week. Increase frequency during weather events, seasonal demand, or emergency-service spikes.
