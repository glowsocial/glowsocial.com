AI can write social media posts now. The question for local business owners isn't whether to use AI — it's **how** to use it without sounding like a robot.

Most AI post generators give you generic content. They don't know your business, your customers, or your brand voice. The result? Posts that could belong to any business in any city.

## The Problem with Generic AI Tools

If you've tried ChatGPT or similar tools for social media, you've probably noticed:

- Posts sound generic and corporate
- You still have to edit, format, and schedule everything
- There's no visual content — just text
- You need to write detailed prompts every time
- It doesn't post for you

The "AI" part saves you 20% of the work. The other 80% — designing graphics, formatting for each platform, scheduling, publishing — still falls on you.

## What a Good AI Post Generator Should Do

A social media AI tool built for local businesses should:

1. **Learn your brand** by reading your actual website
2. **Create complete posts** with captions AND graphics
3. **Format for each platform** (what works on LinkedIn doesn't work on Instagram)
4. **Publish automatically** — not just schedule
5. **Cover multiple platforms** including Google Business Profile
