Posting without a strategy is like throwing darts blindfolded. You might hit something, but you're wasting most of your effort.

## What Content Strategy Actually Means for Local Businesses

It doesn't mean a 30-page marketing document. For a local business, content strategy is simply answering three questions:

1. **What do we post?** (content categories)
2. **When do we post?** (schedule)
3. **Where do we post?** (platforms)

That's it. A strategy isn't complicated — it just needs to be consistent.

## Why Random Posting Fails

Without a strategy, here's what typically happens:

- You post when you remember to (inconsistently)
- You post whatever comes to mind (no theme or purpose)
- You skip platforms that feel hard (missing customers who use them)
- You burn out after 2-3 weeks and stop entirely

The algorithm punishes inconsistency. Your followers forget about you. Your pages look abandoned.

## A Simple Content Strategy for Any Local Business

**Categories:** Rotate between 4-5 types of content:
- Customer stories and reviews
- Tips and advice related to your industry
- Behind-the-scenes looks at your business
- Promotions and offers
- Community and local content

**Schedule:** Post 3-5 times per week. Same days, same approximate times.

**Platforms:** Focus on where your customers are. For most local businesses: Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile.
