# Free social media audit for small business

## Direct Answer
You can audit your small business social media presence for free by using a tool that scans your website, identifies which platforms you're already on, flags which ones you're missing, and generates optimized bios for every network — all in about 60 seconds with no login required.

## Why This Matters
Most small businesses have profiles scattered across platforms in various states of completion. Maybe you set up Facebook two years ago but never touched LinkedIn. Maybe your Instagram bio still mentions a promotion from last summer. A [social media audit](/setup) reveals the gaps — which platforms you're missing entirely, which bios are outdated, and which profiles have mismatched business info. Without an audit, you're guessing. You might be invisible on the exact platform your customers use to validate businesses before calling.

## Real-World Example
A wedding photographer ran a free audit and discovered she had no LinkedIn presence at all — despite 40% of her bookings coming from corporate event referrals by HR managers who naturally search LinkedIn first. She also found that her Google Business Profile description was still showing "Now booking 2024 weddings." Setting up LinkedIn and updating her GBP description took 15 minutes and led to three new corporate inquiries within the first month.

## What Most People Get Wrong
Thinking a social media audit requires a paid consultant or a complicated spreadsheet. You don't need to hire someone to tell you your Facebook About section is blank. A free scan tool will surface 80% of the issues instantly — incomplete profiles, missing platforms, outdated bios, wrong phone numbers. Fix those first. Strategy comes after the basics are in place.
