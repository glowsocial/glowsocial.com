HVAC companies should use Google Business Profile posts for timely, local, high-intent updates: maintenance reminders, emergency availability, tune-up offers, review highlights, and weather-driven service prompts.

## 12 Google Business Profile Post Examples for HVAC

### 1. Seasonal tune-up reminder

AC systems work hardest during the first extreme heat wave. Schedule a tune-up before temperatures spike so small issues do not become emergency calls.

### 2. Emergency availability

No cool air? We have emergency AC repair appointments available this week. Call now and we will help you get back on the schedule.

### 3. Filter reminder

If your AC is running constantly, check the filter first. A clogged filter can reduce airflow and make the system work harder than it should.

### 4. Review highlight

Another local homeowner trusted us with a same-day AC repair. Thank you for the kind review and for choosing our team.

### 5. Maintenance checklist

Before summer: change the filter, clear debris around the outdoor unit, check thermostat settings, and schedule a professional inspection.

### 6. Service area post

Now booking AC tune-ups in Phoenix, Glendale, Peoria, and Scottsdale this week.

### 7. Financing reminder

Replacing an HVAC system is a major decision. Ask about available financing options before approving a repair or replacement.

### 8. Thermostat tip

If rooms feel uneven, the thermostat may not be the only issue. Airflow, ductwork, insulation, and system size can all affect comfort.

### 9. Monsoon or storm reminder

After a dust storm, check the outdoor unit for debris and restricted airflow. If the system sounds different, schedule service.

### 10. Heat pump education

Heat pumps handle both heating and cooling. If performance drops in either season, it is worth checking before peak demand.

### 11. Before-and-after proof

This older unit was replaced with a more efficient system built for the home's current cooling needs.

### 12. Booking prompt

Appointments fill quickly during the first hot week. Book maintenance now instead of waiting for an emergency repair.

## Best GBP Cadence for HVAC

Post 1-2 times per week during normal months. Increase to 2-3 times per week during high-demand periods:

- First summer heat wave.
- First winter cold snap.
- Monsoon or storm season.
- Spring and fall tune-up windows.

## Why GBP Matters for HVAC

HVAC searches are often urgent. A homeowner with no cool air is not browsing for entertainment. They are checking who looks active, credible, available, and nearby. A current Google Business Profile post can be the difference between "this company looks alive" and "I am not sure they are still active."
