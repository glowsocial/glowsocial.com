## What Hootsuite Does Well

Hootsuite is a comprehensive platform with features that matter for larger organizations:


<ul>
<li><strong>Unified inbox:</strong> Manage comments and messages from all platforms in one place</li>
<li><strong>Advanced analytics:</strong> Detailed reporting on performance and ROI</li>
<li><strong>Team collaboration:</strong> Approval workflows, role permissions, assignment</li>
<li><strong>Social listening:</strong> Monitor mentions and conversations</li>
<li><strong>Paid ad management:</strong> Run ads across platforms</li>
<li><strong>Enterprise integrations:</strong> CRM, customer service tools, etc.</li>
</ul>

<strong>Best for:</strong> Marketing teams, agencies, and enterprises who need robust management tools.


<strong>The challenge for small businesses:</strong> It's expensive ($99+/month minimum for useful features) and requires significant time to use effectively. Most of Hootsuite's features are overkill for a small business just trying to stay visible on social media.
