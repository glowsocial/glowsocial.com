## Current Reality: How Much Time Small Businesses Actually Spend

<strong>Common patterns:</strong>

<ul>
<li><strong>Overcommitters:</strong> 10+ hours/month, often burning out after a few months</li>
<li><strong>Inconsistent posters:</strong> 0-5 hours on random weeks, nothing for months between</li>
<li><strong>Avoiders:</strong> 0 hours—profiles abandoned or minimal activity</li>
<li><strong>Efficient maintainers:</strong> 2-4 hours/month through batching</li>
</ul>

Most small businesses either overdo it until burnout or avoid it entirely. Neither is sustainable.


## What You Actually Need

For baseline social media visibility (staying visible, not going viral):


<ul>
<li><strong>Posting frequency:</strong> 3x per week (12 posts/month)</li>
<li><strong>Engagement:</strong> 5-10 minutes daily responding to comments/DMs</li>
<li><strong>Total time:</strong> 3-5 hours/month (if you do it yourself)</li>
</ul>

That's it. You don't need to post daily. You don't need hours of engagement. Just consistent visibility.

## How to Spend Your Social Media Time Efficiently

If you're doing it yourself, here's the efficient breakdown:


<strong>Monthly batching session (2-3 hours once per month):</strong>

<ul>
<li>Plan 12 posts for the month (30 minutes)</li>
<li>Write all captions (45 minutes)</li>
<li>Create or gather all images (45 minutes)</li>
<li>Schedule everything (30 minutes)</li>
</ul>

<strong>Ongoing engagement (5-10 minutes daily):</strong>

<ul>
<li>Check for comments and messages</li>
<li>Respond to any engagement</li>
<li>This is the highest-value social media activity</li>
</ul>

## What NOT to Spend Time On

Many small businesses waste time on low-value activities:


<strong>Skip or minimize:</strong>

<ul>
<li>Obsessing over analytics (for small businesses, basic visibility matters more than optimization)</li>
<li>Trying to go viral (focus on your actual customers, not everyone)</li>
<li>Creating perfect graphics (phone photos often work better)</li>
<li>Posting more than once per day (diminishing returns)</li>
<li>Being on every platform (pick 2-3 and focus)</li>
</ul>
