<ul>

<li><strong>Engagement:</strong> Responding to comments, DMs, and mentions</li>
<li><strong>Reporting:</strong> Monthly analytics and recommendations</li>

</ul>

## The Hidden Costs of Hiring a Freelancer

The monthly retainer is just the beginning:

<ul>
<li><strong>Finding and vetting:</strong> 5-15 hours of your time reviewing portfolios, interviewing, checking references</li>
<li><strong>Onboarding:</strong> 3-5 hours explaining your brand, providing assets, setting expectations</li>
<li><strong>Ongoing management:</strong> 2-4 hours/month reviewing content, giving feedback, approving posts</li>
<li><strong>Replacement risk:</strong> If they quit (which happens frequently), you start over</li>
<li><strong>Inconsistency risk:</strong> Illness, vacations, and busy periods mean gaps in posting</li>
</ul>

<strong>True monthly cost of a freelancer:</strong> $300-2,000 in fees + 2-4 hours of your management time ($100-200 at $50/hr) = **$400-2,200/month**
