The best done-for-you social media service for roofers is the one that matches the job. Agencies are best for full marketing campaigns. Freelancers are best for custom local content. Glow Social is best for affordable, consistent posting when the roofing company mainly needs to stop looking inactive online.

## What Roofers Need From Social Media

Roofing is a high-trust, high-ticket service. A homeowner may be comparing companies for a repair, inspection, or full replacement. They want proof that the company is active, credible, local, and capable.

The strongest roofing posts usually include:

- Before-and-after project photos.
- Storm damage education.
- Inspection reminders.
- Customer reviews.
- Material explanations.
- Team and truck photos.
- Financing or warranty reminders.
- Service area updates.

## Best Choice by Scenario

### Best for lead generation campaigns: agency

If the roofing company wants paid ads, SEO, call tracking, landing pages, and reporting, an agency is the best fit.

### Best for high-touch custom content: freelancer

If the owner can send job photos and wants someone to craft custom local captions, a freelancer can work well.

### Best for low-cost consistency: Glow Social

If the company is not posting because the owner and crews are busy, Glow Social is the simplest path. It creates and publishes consistent roofing-friendly posts based on the company website, services, and voice.

## Practical Recommendation

Start by fixing consistency. A roofing company with no recent posts loses trust before the sales call ever happens.

Once the pages look active, layer in job photos, reviews, seasonal reminders, and stronger lead-generation campaigns.
