## The Consistency Problem

Here's what usually happens: you decide to get serious about social media. You post for two or three weeks. Then business picks up, life happens, and your pages go quiet for months.

That inconsistency is worse than not posting at all. An abandoned social media page tells potential customers you might not be in business anymore.

## The Options

**Do it yourself with Buffer (free to $15/month):**
Schedule posts you create. You'll spend 3-5 hours per month on content creation, design, and scheduling.

**Manage it with Hootsuite ($99+/month):**
Enterprise platform with analytics, social listening, and team features. Powerful but complex for small businesses.

**Automate it with Glow Social ($49/month):**
Glow Social creates and publishes 12+ posts per month automatically. Setup takes 5 minutes, and posts are customized to your business.

## Bottom Line

Buffer is for DIY creators. Hootsuite is for marketing teams. Glow Social is for business owners who want posting handled.
