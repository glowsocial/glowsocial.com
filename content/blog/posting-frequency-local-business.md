One of the most common questions local businesses ask: How often should I post on social media? The short answer: <strong>3 posts per week is enough for most small businesses</strong>. Consistency matters more than frequency, and posting so much that you burn out helps no one.


Here's what the data and experience actually show.

## For Most Local Businesses: 3x/Week Is Fine

Despite what marketing gurus claim, you don't need to post every day. For most local businesses—restaurants, salons, contractors, retail—3 posts per week across your main platforms is enough to:


<ul>
<li>Stay visible in followers' feeds</li>
<li>Maintain algorithm favor</li>
<li>Build recognition over time</li>
<li>Keep your profiles active</li>
</ul>

That's about 12 posts per month—exactly what Glow Social provides for $49/month automatically.


## Quality and Consistency > Frequency

Here's what research consistently shows:


<strong>Consistent 3x/week beats sporadic daily posting.</strong> An account that posts 3 times per week every week builds more visibility than one that posts daily for two weeks then disappears for a month.


<strong>People don't notice if you post 3x or 5x per week.</strong> Your followers aren't counting. They just want to see you occasionally when they scroll.


<strong>More posts doesn't equal more reach.</strong> Algorithms don't reward volume—they reward engagement. Two posts that get good engagement beat ten posts no one interacts with.

## When More Frequent Posting Makes Sense

Some situations warrant higher frequency:


<ul>
<li><strong>You're growing aggressively:</strong> New businesses or major growth phases benefit from more visibility</li>
<li><strong>Your business is content-native:</strong> Restaurants with daily specials, retailers with constant new inventory</li>
<li><strong>You have help:</strong> A team member dedicated to social media</li>
<li><strong>You genuinely enjoy it:</strong> Creating content energizes rather than drains you</li>
</ul>

If none of these apply, 3x/week is fine.


## When Less Frequent Posting Is Okay

You might get away with 2x/week if:


<ul>
<li>Your posts consistently get good engagement</li>
<li>You have other strong marketing channels (referrals, Google, etc.)</li>
<li>Your industry moves slowly</li>
</ul>

Below 2x/week, you risk looking inactive or abandoned.

## Why Consistency Beats Everything

The single most important factor isn't how often you post—it's whether you keep posting.


Thousands of local businesses have tried social media like this:

<ul>
<li>Start with enthusiasm, post daily</li>
<li>Get busy with work, posting drops off</li>
<li>Stop for weeks or months</li>
<li>Start over, repeat the cycle</li>
</ul>

This pattern is worse than posting 2-3x/week consistently forever.
