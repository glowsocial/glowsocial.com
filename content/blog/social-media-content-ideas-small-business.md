Running out of ideas for what to post? You're not alone — **"what should I post on social media?"** is the #1 question small business owners ask about marketing. Here are 47 ideas you can steal today, organized by category so you can build a full month of content in one sitting.


## Educational Content (Tips & How-Tos)

These establish you as the expert in your field:

1. **"3 things most people don't know about [your industry]"**
2. **Quick tip of the week** — one actionable piece of advice
3. **"Here's why [common misconception] is wrong"** — myth-busting
4. **Step-by-step process** of how you do something
5. **"Questions to ask before hiring a [your profession]"**
6. **Seasonal advice** — what your customers should do this month
7. **"The difference between [option A] and [option B]"** — comparison content
8. **Tool or product recommendation** you use in your business
9. **"If I were starting over, here's what I'd do differently"**
10. **Answer a real question** a customer asked you this week


## Behind-the-Scenes Content

These make your business feel human and approachable:

11. **Your morning routine** — how you start a typical workday
12. **Team spotlight** — introduce one team member
13. **"Day in the life"** of running your business
14. **Your workspace** — show where the work happens
15. **Packing an order or prepping for a job** — the process
16. **"What I'm working on this week"** — current projects
17. **Your tools of the trade** — equipment, software, supplies
18. **A business milestone** — anniversary, customer count, new hire
19. **"What nobody tells you about running a [business type]"**
20. **Show your commute, job site, or workspace setup**


## Social Proof & Credibility

These build trust and convert followers into customers:

21. **Customer review screenshot** with a thank-you caption
22. **Before and after** — the transformation you created
23. **Case study** — specific results you achieved for a client
24. **"Here's what [customer name] said about working with us"**
25. **Awards, certifications, or continuing education** you've completed
26. **Press mentions or features** — "We were featured in..."
27. **Project completion photo** — the finished result
28. **Customer milestone** — celebrating their success
29. **Google review response** — show how you engage with feedback
30. **Year-in-review stats** — jobs completed, customers served


## Engagement & Community Content

These get people talking and boost your algorithm reach:

31. **"This or that?"** — give two options, ask followers to pick
32. **Poll or question** — "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
33. **"Caption this"** — fun photo from your business
34. **Fill in the blank** — "The best thing about living in [your city] is ___"
35. **"Tell me you're a [your customer type] without telling me"**
36. **Local shout-out** — tag and promote a neighboring business
37. **Holiday or awareness day** tie-in relevant to your industry
38. **"What should we name [new product/service/mascot]?"**
39. **"Agree or disagree?"** — share a strong opinion about your industry
40. **Throwback** — your first job, first customer, early days


## Promotional Content (Use Sparingly)

These drive action — but keep them under 20% of your total posts:

41. **New service or product announcement**
42. **Limited-time offer or seasonal special**
43. **"Here's how to book/order/get started"** — clear CTA
44. **"We have availability this [week/month]"** — urgency
45. **Referral program** — reward customers who send friends
46. **Gift card or package deal** — especially before holidays
47. **"Here's what $X gets you"** — show the value at a specific price point


## How to Use These Ideas

**The batching method:** Pick 12 ideas from this list (mix the categories). Write all 12 captions in one sitting. Schedule them. Done for the month.

**The rotation method:** Use this weekly template:
- **Monday:** Educational (#1-10)
- **Wednesday:** Behind-the-scenes (#11-20)
- **Friday:** Social proof (#21-30) or engagement (#31-40)
