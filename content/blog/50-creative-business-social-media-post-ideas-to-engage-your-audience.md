Every business owner has stared at a blank posting screen thinking, "What do I even say?" Here are 50 ideas organized by category so you never run out of content.

## Behind the Scenes (1-10)

1. Show your workspace before customers arrive
2. Introduce a team member and share what they do
3. Share your morning routine for opening up
4. Film a time-lapse of a project or service being completed
5. Show what your desk or dashboard looks like on a busy day
6. Share the tools of your trade
7. Post a "day in the life" series
8. Show how you prepare for a big project
9. Share a packaging or delivery prep process
10. Give a tour of your space

## Customer Spotlights (11-20)

11. Share a customer review as a graphic post
12. Post a before-and-after photo (with permission)
13. Highlight a long-time customer
14. Share a customer's kind message or email
15. Post a thank-you message after a milestone (100th customer, etc.)
16. Share how a customer found you
17. Post a case study of a particularly great result
18. Share a customer's referral story
19. Highlight a customer's business (if B2B)
20. Post a "customer of the month" feature

## Tips and Advice (21-30)

21. Share one tip related to your industry
22. Post a "did you know?" fact about your field
23. Create a simple how-to related to your service
24. Share seasonal maintenance tips
25. Post common mistakes customers make (and how to avoid them)
26. Answer a frequently asked question
27. Share a myth vs. fact about your industry
28. Post a checklist for customers to use
29. Share a quick tutorial video
30. Offer a seasonal preparation guide

## Promotions and Offers (31-35)

31. Announce a seasonal promotion
32. Share a referral program
33. Post a limited-time offer
34. Highlight a new service or product
35. Share bundle or package deals

## Community and Local (36-40)

36. Highlight a local event you're participating in
37. Give a shout-out to another local business
38. Share local news related to your industry
39. Post about a community cause you support
40. Share your involvement in local charity or volunteer work

## Educational Content (41-45)

41. Explain how your service works
42. Break down the difference between two services you offer
43. Share industry trends that affect your customers
44. Post about certifications or training your team has completed
45. Explain your process from start to finish

## Fun and Engagement (46-50)

46. Post a "this or that" poll
47. Share a throwback photo of your business
48. Ask followers to caption a photo
49. Run a simple giveaway or contest
50. Post a fun holiday-related message
