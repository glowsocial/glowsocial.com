## The Core Difference: Recycling vs. Creating

**SocialBee's approach:** You build a content library. You create posts, organize them into categories (tips, promotions, behind-the-scenes), and SocialBee rotates through them automatically. Great content gets reshared. AI helps you write captions faster.

**Glow Social's approach:** You don't build anything. AI scans your website, understands your business, and creates 12 original posts per month with images. Published automatically.

**The key question:** Do you already have content to recycle?

- **Yes, I have 50+ posts worth resharing** → SocialBee makes sense
- **No, I'm starting from zero** → SocialBee has nothing to recycle; you need Glow Social

## When SocialBee Is the Better Choice

SocialBee is genuinely good if you:

- **Already create social media content** and want a smarter way to organize it
- **Have evergreen content** that's worth resharing (tips, guides, quotes)
- **Want to post on 8+ platforms** including X, Pinterest, Bluesky, Google Business
- **Enjoy the content creation process** and want AI to speed it up
- **Manage multiple brands** — SocialBee's workspace system handles this well
- **Want detailed analytics** with up to 2 years of historical data

**Bottom line:** SocialBee is an excellent tool for people who already do social media and want to do it more efficiently.

## When Glow Social Is the Better Choice

Glow Social is the better choice if you:

- **Don't have existing content** to recycle
- **Don't have 4-6 hours/month** to create content and manage categories
- **Want social media completely hands-off** — no content library to build
- **Just need professional, consistent posting** without creative control
- **Have been meaning to post but never do** — the problem is getting started, not organizing
- **Want the simplest possible solution** — one plan, one price, no decisions

**Bottom line:** If social media feels like a chore you keep putting off, Glow Social removes the chore entirely.
