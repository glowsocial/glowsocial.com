For local businesses—restaurants, salons, contractors, retail—<strong>Google Business Profile often drives more leads than social media</strong>. When someone searches "plumber near me" or "best coffee shop in Scottsdale," your GBP listing is what appears. Social media builds brand awareness and trust over time.

The answer isn't one or the other. It's both, with the right priorities.
