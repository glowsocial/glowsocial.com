## DIY vs Automated: Fitness Studio Reality Check

<strong>DIY works if:</strong>

<ul>
<li>You have staff who can handle content creation</li>
<li>Trainers are comfortable creating videos and posts</li>
<li>Someone can dedicate 3-5 hours monthly to marketing</li>
</ul>

<strong>Automated posting works if:</strong>

<ul>
<li>Owners and trainers are too busy with clients for content</li>
<li>You've tried posting consistently but it always drops off</li>
<li>Slow seasons hurt because you didn't post during busy seasons</li>
<li>Marketing always falls to the bottom of the to-do list</li>
</ul>
