## The Core Difference: Owner vs. Agency

There's a simple test to determine which tool is for you:

- **"I manage other people's social media"** → Sendible
- **"I need my own social media managed"** → Glow Social

Sendible is designed for the person who manages social media as a profession. Glow Social is designed for the person who wants to avoid social media as a task.

## When Sendible Is the Better Choice

Sendible genuinely excels if you:

- **Run a social media agency** — client dashboards and approval workflows are top-tier
- **Manage 5+ accounts** — bulk scheduling and multi-brand management
- **Need client-facing reports** — white-label PDF reports you can brand
- **Have a team** — role-based permissions, approval chains, and collaboration
- **Want WordPress integration** — publish blog posts alongside social content
- **Need a social inbox** — all messages and mentions in one place with team assignment
- **Anticipate growth** — scales from 6 to 400+ profiles

**Bottom line:** Sendible is one of the best agency tools on the market. If you manage social media for clients, it's hard to beat.

## When Glow Social Is the Better Choice

Glow Social makes more sense if you:

- **Own a business** and social media is one of dozens of things you manage
- **Don't have a team** — approval workflows and collaboration features don't help
- **Don't manage client accounts** — agency features add complexity, not value
- **Don't create content** — a scheduling tool with no content is an empty calendar
- **Want the simplest possible solution** — one price, one setup, done

**Bottom line:** Sendible gives you the tools to be a social media professional. Glow Social gives you results without being one.

## The Honest Take on Sendible

Sendible doesn't get mentioned as often as Hootsuite or Buffer, but it's arguably the best value for agencies. The client dashboard system, approval workflows, and white-label reporting are more polished than many tools twice its price.

But everything that makes Sendible great for agencies makes it overkill for a solo business owner. Client dashboards when you have no clients. Approval workflows when there's no one to approve. Team collaboration when there's no team. You're paying for infrastructure you don't use.

## Bottom Line

**Choose Sendible if:** You manage social media for multiple clients or have a team. It's excellent value for agencies.
