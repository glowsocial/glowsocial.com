Roofers should post Facebook content that reduces homeowner risk: before-and-after project photos, storm damage reminders, inspection tips, reviews, financing reminders, warranty explanations, and service-area updates.

## Facebook Post Ideas for Roofers

1. Before-and-after roof replacement.
2. Storm inspection reminder.
3. Customer review.
4. Signs of missing shingles.
5. What to do after hail.
6. Material comparison.
7. Financing explanation.
8. Warranty reminder.
9. Crew or truck photo.
10. Service-area post.
11. Insurance claim education.
12. Seasonal maintenance checklist.
13. "How long does replacement take?"
14. Roof leak warning signs.
15. Recent project story.

## Weekly Mix

Post one education item, one proof item, and one timely reminder each week. During storm season, add more inspection and availability posts.
