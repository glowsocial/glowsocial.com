Text-only posts get scrolled past. Posts with professional images get noticed. For local businesses, the quality of your social media graphics directly impacts how customers perceive your brand.

## Why Images Matter More Than Copy

On social media, you have about 1.5 seconds to catch someone's attention. A professional graphic does that. A wall of text doesn't.

Studies consistently show that posts with images get 2-3x more engagement than text-only posts across every major platform.

## The Image Problem for Local Businesses

Most local businesses face one of three scenarios:

1. **No images at all** — text-only posts that get zero engagement
2. **Low-quality photos** — blurry phone shots that actually hurt your brand
3. **Generic stock photos** — professional but impersonal, making your business look like everyone else

The solution is branded graphics — professional images that match your brand colors, include your messaging, and look like they were designed by a marketing team.
