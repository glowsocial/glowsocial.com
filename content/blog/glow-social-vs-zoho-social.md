## When Zoho Social Is the Better Choice

Zoho Social is genuinely excellent if you:

- **Already use Zoho CRM** — the integration is seamless and powerful
- **Have a team member who does social media** — give them the cheapest professional tool
- **Need a free plan** to start with — Zoho's free tier is legitimate
- **Want social listening** — monitor brand mentions and conversations
- **Manage multiple platforms** including X, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Business
- **Want the lowest possible subscription cost** and have time for content creation
- **Need CRM-connected social media** — see social interactions alongside customer data

**Bottom line:** If you're a Zoho ecosystem user with a team member who creates content, Zoho Social is probably the best value in the market.

## When Glow Social Is the Better Choice

Glow Social makes more sense if you:

- **Don't use Zoho CRM** — the integration advantage disappears
- **Don't have a team member for social media** — you're the owner doing everything
- **Don't have 5-10 hours/month** for content creation
- **Don't need CRM integration or social listening** — you just need consistent posting
- **Value your time at more than $5/hour** — the math favors done-for-you

**Bottom line:** If the problem is "I don't have time to create content," a cheaper scheduling tool doesn't solve it.

## The Honest Take on Zoho Social

Zoho Social doesn't get enough credit. At $10/month, it offers features that competitors charge $50-100 for. The Zoho CRM integration is unmatched. The free plan is genuinely usable. If your business runs on Zoho, adding Zoho Social is a no-brainer.

But like every scheduling tool, it assumes you'll create the content. For business owners who won't (or can't), the $10/month plus 8 hours of content creation time is a worse deal than $99/month with everything handled.

## Bottom Line

**Choose Zoho Social if:** You use Zoho CRM, have time for content creation, and want the cheapest scheduling tool.
**Choose Glow Social if:** You don't have time for content creation and want it done automatically.
