Before comparing social media tools, some business owners ask a more fundamental question: **do I even need social media?**

The short answer: yes, but not for the reasons most marketers will tell you. You don't need viral content or thousands of followers. You need <strong>proof of life</strong> — an active presence that tells potential customers you're real, open, and professional.

## The Real Cost of Not Posting

Empty social media pages don't just fail to attract customers — they actively repel them:

<ul>
<li><strong>78% of consumers</strong> research businesses on social media before purchasing</li>
<li>An inactive page makes prospects wonder if you're still in business</li>
<li>Competitors with active pages win the trust comparison by default</li>
<li>Referrals die when there's nothing shareable</li>
</ul>

The cost isn't in what you spend on social media. It's in the customers you lose because your pages look abandoned.

## You Don't Need to Be a Content Creator

Here's the good news: effective social media for local businesses doesn't require:

<ul>
<li>Going viral</li>
<li>Dancing on TikTok</li>
<li>Posting every day</li>
<li>Becoming an influencer</li>
<li>Spending hours on content</li>
</ul>

It requires: **consistent, professional posts that show you're active.** That's it.
