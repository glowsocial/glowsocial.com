## Direct Answer

Glow Social can create a full month of posts from a local business website: educational tips, customer FAQs, service reminders, reviews, authority posts, Google Business Profile updates, and seasonal content tailored to the business.

## Example Monthly Calendar

Here is a realistic 12-post month for a local service business.

### 1. Customer FAQ

Wondering how often to schedule maintenance? For most homes, once a year is enough to catch small issues before they become expensive repairs.

### 2. Review post

Another local customer trusted us with same-week service. Thank you for choosing our team and for the kind review.

### 3. Service reminder

If it has been more than a year since your last inspection, now is a good time to get on the calendar before peak season.

### 4. Behind-the-scenes

Clean work matters. We protect the workspace, explain what we are doing, and leave the area better than we found it.

### 5. Educational tip

Small warning signs often show up before a major issue. Unusual sounds, smells, leaks, or slow performance are worth checking early.

### 6. Google Business Profile update

Now booking appointments in our local service area this week. Call or request a time online.

### 7. Myth-busting post

Waiting until something breaks usually costs more than preventive maintenance. A quick check can save a bigger repair later.

### 8. Team trust post

When you call, you get a local team that knows the area, the common issues, and the standards customers expect.

### 9. Seasonal post

Season changes are the best time to check systems that work harder during extreme weather.

### 10. Process explanation

Our first step is always diagnosis. We identify the actual cause before recommending a repair or replacement.

### 11. Offer post

Appointments are available this week for inspections and routine service. Book now before the schedule fills.

### 12. Authority post

The cheapest fix is not always the best fix. We explain the options so customers can choose what makes sense for their home and budget.

## Why These Posts Work

The calendar mixes four content types:

- Education.
- Proof.
- Behind-the-scenes trust.
- Clear service reminders.

That mix keeps the business visible without making every post a sales pitch.
