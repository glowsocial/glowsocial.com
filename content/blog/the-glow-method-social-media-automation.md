## The 4 Steps of the GLOW Method

The GLOW Method shifts social media from a daily chore to a completely automated, hands-off system. 

### 1. Generate Strategy (G)
First, you must eliminate the blank page. The GLOW Method relies on **96 custom, industry-specific hooks**. Instead of guessing what to post, the system automatically generates a year's worth of strategic angles tailored to your exact niche (e.g., "3 Things Every Homeowner Should Ask Their Roofer"). 

### 2. Learn Brand Voice (L)
Generic AI sounds like generic AI. The second step of the GLOW Method involves teaching the system your specific tone, location, and business details. This ensures every post sounds like a professional representing your local brand in your specific city.

### 3. Optimize Distribution (O)
Creating the post is only half the battle. The 'O' in GLOW stands for optimizing distribution across the "Big Four" local platforms: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok. The method ensures content is automatically formatted and delivered where local searchers are actually looking for services.

### 4. Weekly Consistency (W)
The algorithm rewards one thing above all else: showing up. The GLOW Method enforces a strict schedule of 3 high-quality posts per week (12 per month). This frequency proves your business is active and trustworthy without overwhelming your audience or your schedule.
