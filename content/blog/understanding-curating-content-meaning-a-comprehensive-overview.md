Content curation means finding and sharing relevant content from other sources — rather than creating everything from scratch. For local businesses, it's a way to stay active on social media even when you don't have time to create original content.

## How Content Curation Works

Instead of writing every post yourself, you share valuable content from:
- Industry publications
- Local news sources
- Partner businesses
- Relevant experts
- Community organizations

You add your own perspective or commentary, and it becomes a post that provides value to your audience.

## Benefits of Content Curation

**Saves time:** Sharing existing content is faster than creating from scratch.

**Positions you as an expert:** By curating the best industry content, you show customers you're knowledgeable and connected.

**Builds relationships:** Sharing other businesses' content strengthens local partnerships.

**Keeps your feed active:** Curated content fills gaps between your original posts.

## Curation vs. Creation for Local Businesses

The best social media strategies mix both:
- **60% original content** — your work, your team, your services
- **20% curated content** — industry tips, local news, relevant articles
- **20% engagement content** — questions, polls, and community interaction
