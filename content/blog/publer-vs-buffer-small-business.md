Publer and Buffer are both affordable social media scheduling tools. They are also both easy to misunderstand.

They can help you organize and publish posts. They do not remove the need to create the posts.


Choose **Buffer** if you want the simplest scheduler for a small number of channels.

Choose **Publer** if you want more flexible social account/member pricing, broader workflow features, bulk scheduling, AI tools, and a more feature-rich publishing setup.

Choose **neither** if your problem is that you do not have finished posts to schedule.

## Buffer: Best for Simple Scheduling

Buffer is the cleaner, simpler option.

It works well if:

- You want a low-learning-curve scheduler.
- You manage a few profiles.
- You do not need complex workflows.
- You want the free plan to test the habit first.
- You create your own posts.

Buffer's free plan supports up to 3 channels and 10 scheduled posts per channel. During this June 2026 refresh, Buffer's public pricing page listed Essentials from $5/month per channel billed yearly and Team from $10/month per channel billed yearly.

That makes Buffer easy to understand for small setups.

## Publer: Best for More Scheduling Flexibility

Publer works well if:

- You manage more social profiles.
- You want bulk scheduling.
- You want AI assistance and post workflows.
- You want collaboration or approval features.
- You want pricing that scales by social accounts and members.

Publer's public plans page says the free plan includes 1 user, 1 workspace, 3 social accounts excluding Twitter/X, and 10 scheduled posts per account. Publer's paid plans scale by social accounts and additional members, which can be useful when Buffer's per-channel structure does not match the way your profiles are set up.

That can be attractive if Buffer's per-channel model does not fit the way your profiles are structured.

## Supported Channels

Both tools support the major platforms small businesses care about.

Buffer supports platforms including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Business Profile, Threads, X, Bluesky, and Mastodon.

Publer supports Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Google Business locations, YouTube, WordPress, TikTok, Telegram, Mastodon, Threads, and Bluesky.

If Google Business Profile matters to you, both are worth considering.

## The Real Difference for Small Businesses

The real difference is not only price or channel support. It is complexity.

Buffer is simpler. Publer is more feature-rich.

That means:

- Buffer is better if you want less to manage.
- Publer is better if you want more control.
- Neither is better if the calendar is empty.
