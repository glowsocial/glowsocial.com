Sometimes you need a reminder about why social media matters for your business. Here are quotes from marketers, business leaders, and industry experts — with practical takeaways for local businesses.

## On Consistency

> "The best time to post on social media was yesterday. The next best time is today."

**Takeaway:** Don't wait for perfect content. Start posting consistently and improve over time.

> "Social media is a marathon, not a sprint."

**Takeaway:** One viral post won't build your business. Showing up regularly will.

## On Authenticity

> "People do not buy goods and services. They buy relations, stories, and magic." — Seth Godin

**Takeaway:** Your social media should show the real people and real work behind your business.

> "Authenticity is the daily practice of letting go of who we think we're supposed to be and embracing who we are." — Brené Brown

**Takeaway:** Generic, polished marketing content isn't what local customers want. They want to see YOUR business.

## On Value

> "Content is fire. Social media is gasoline." — Jay Baer

**Takeaway:** Good content gets amplified by social media. Bad content stays invisible no matter how many platforms you post to.

> "In a world full of ads, be a conversation."

**Takeaway:** Ask questions, respond to comments, and treat social media as a two-way conversation.

## On Time Management

> "You don't need more time. You need a system."

**Takeaway:** The business owners who succeed on social media aren't spending more hours — they're using better systems.
