## Direct Answer

Glow Social supports 12 platforms: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, X (Twitter), Google Business Profile, Threads, Bluesky, and more. You can connect unlimited social accounts and schedule content across all platforms from a single dashboard. Each post is adapted to the platform's format and best practices.

## Why This Matters

Multi-platform posting is where most small business owners lose time. Writing a post for Instagram, then rewriting it for LinkedIn's professional tone, then adjusting the format for Facebook, and then creating a different version for TikTok turns one piece of content into four separate tasks. Glow Social generates platform-appropriate content for each channel, adjusting tone, length, and format automatically.

## Real-World Example

A fitness studio owner was active only on Instagram because managing more platforms felt impossible alongside training clients and running the business. After connecting all four platforms to Glow Social, she started appearing on Facebook (where her 40-plus clientele spends time), LinkedIn (where corporate wellness partnerships originate), and TikTok (where younger prospects discover local fitness options), with zero additional effort. Within 60 days, she booked her first corporate wellness contract through a LinkedIn connection who had been seeing her posts.

## What Most People Get Wrong

Being on more platforms doesn't mean more work if the content is generated for you. The risk is actually being on too few platforms. If your ideal customer is on LinkedIn but you only post on Instagram, you're invisible to them.
