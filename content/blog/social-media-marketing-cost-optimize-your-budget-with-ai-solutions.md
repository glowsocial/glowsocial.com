The cost of social media marketing ranges from $0 to $10,000+ per month depending on how you approach it. Here's an honest breakdown.

## Social Media Marketing Costs by Approach

### DIY with Free Tools: $0/month
**What you get:** Free scheduling with limited features
**What it costs you:** 10-15 hours per month of your time
**Reality check:** If your time is worth $50/hour, it's actually costing you $500-750/month

### DIY with Paid Tools: $20-100/month
**What you get:** Better scheduling, analytics, and some AI features (Buffer, Hootsuite, Later)
**What it costs you:** 8-12 hours/month — tools save some time but you still create the content
**Reality check:** $20 for the tool + $400-600 of your time = $420-620 real cost

### Freelance Social Media Manager: $300-1,500/month
**What you get:** Someone else creates content and posts for you
**What it costs you:** Managing the relationship, providing direction, reviewing content
**Reality check:** Quality varies wildly and you still spend 2-4 hours/month managing them

### Done-for-You Automation (Glow Social): $49/month
**What you get:** AI reads your website, creates custom content with graphics, publishes to 13 platforms
**What it costs you:** 5 minutes setup, 5 minutes monthly to approve posts
**Reality check:** Lowest cost, least time, most consistent output

### Marketing Agency: $2,000-10,000/month
**What you get:** Full-service marketing including social media, ads, strategy, and reporting
**What it costs you:** Monthly meetings, direction, and a significant budget
**Reality check:** Overkill for most local businesses under $1M revenue
