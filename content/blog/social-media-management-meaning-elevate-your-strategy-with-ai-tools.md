Social media management is the process of creating, scheduling, publishing, and monitoring content across social media platforms. For local businesses, it's how you stay visible to customers who spend hours every day on their phones.

## What Social Media Management Includes

**Content creation:** Writing captions, designing graphics, and creating videos that represent your brand.

**Scheduling and publishing:** Posting content at optimal times across multiple platforms.

**Community management:** Responding to comments, messages, and reviews.

**Analytics and reporting:** Tracking what's working and adjusting your approach.

**Strategy:** Deciding what to post, when, and where based on your business goals.

## Why It Matters for Local Businesses

Your customers are on social media. When they need a plumber, a dentist, a restaurant, or a contractor, they check online first. Your social media presence is often their first impression.

An active, professional social media presence says: "We're open, we're busy, and we're good at what we do."

An empty or outdated profile says: "We might be out of business."

## DIY vs. Professional Management

**DIY:** Free but costs 10-15 hours/month of your time. Most business owners quit after 2-3 weeks.

**Freelancer:** $300-1,500/month. Quality varies and you still manage the relationship.

**Agency:** $2,000+/month. Full-service but expensive for most local businesses.
