## What $300-500/Month Gets You (Freelancer)

A basic freelance social media manager package typically includes:


<ul>
<li>8-12 posts per month</li>
<li>Coverage of 2-3 platforms</li>
<li>Basic graphics (usually Canva-created)</li>
<li>Content calendar planning</li>
<li>Monthly check-in call</li>
</ul>

<strong>What you don't get:</strong> Engagement/community management, paid advertising, comprehensive strategy.


<strong>Best for:</strong> Businesses wanting human creativity at a reasonable price point.


## What $500-1,000/Month Gets You (Comprehensive Freelancer)

Higher-end freelancers add:


<ul>
<li>15-20 posts per month</li>
<li>Story creation and management</li>
<li>Basic comment monitoring and engagement</li>
<li>Monthly analytics reporting</li>
<li>More polished graphics and occasional video content</li>
</ul>

<strong>Best for:</strong> Businesses that need some engagement management and more content volume.


## What $1,000-3,000/Month Gets You (Agency)

Social media agencies provide team-based service:


<ul>
<li>20-30+ posts per month</li>
<li>Full strategy development</li>
<li>Professional graphics and video content</li>
<li>Community management</li>
<li>Comprehensive analytics and reporting</li>
<li>Dedicated account manager</li>
</ul>

<strong>What you might also get:</strong> Paid advertising management, influencer coordination, crisis management.


<strong>Best for:</strong> Larger businesses with marketing budget and need for comprehensive support.


## Hidden Costs to Consider

Beyond the sticker price, factor in:


<strong>Your time:</strong>

<ul>
<li>DIY tools: 5-10 hours/month</li>
<li>AI software (Glow Social): preview first, then review and approve</li>
<li>Freelancer: 1-2 hours/month for calls and approvals</li>
<li>Agency: 1-2 hours/month for meetings and reviews</li>
</ul>

<strong>Hiring costs:</strong>

<ul>
<li>Finding a good freelancer: 5-10 hours of interviews and trials</li>
<li>Onboarding: 3-5 hours explaining your business</li>
<li>If they leave: Repeat the process</li>
</ul>

<strong>DIY tool stack:</strong>

<ul>
<li>Scheduling tool: $15-50/month</li>
<li>Design tool: $13/month</li>
<li>AI writing tool: $99/month</li>
<li>Total for DIY: $77-112/month + your time</li>
</ul>
