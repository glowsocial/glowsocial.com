## The Core Difference: Data vs. Delegation

**Metricool** gives you the best possible view of your social media performance. You see what's working, what isn't, how competitors are doing, and when to post. Then you create and schedule the content.

**Glow Social** skips all of that. No dashboards, no competitor comparisons, no optimal posting time analysis. Just 12 professional posts per month, published automatically.

These tools answer different questions:
- **Metricool answers:** "How is my social media performing?" → Requires you to do social media first
- **Glow Social answers:** "Can someone just do social media for me?" → Does social media for you

## When Metricool Is the Better Choice

Metricool is genuinely excellent if you:

- **Run paid social campaigns** — Facebook/Google Ads analytics in one dashboard
- **Want competitor analysis** — track up to 100 competitor profiles
- **Need reporting for clients** — downloadable PDF/PPT reports
- **Manage multiple brands** — up to 5 brands for $18/month
- **Want a free analytics tool** — the free plan is legitimately useful
- **Already create content** and want to understand what performs best
- **Track web traffic alongside social** — unified marketing analytics
- **Use Twitch** — one of the few tools with Twitch integration

**Bottom line:** If you're a marketer who loves data and already creates content, Metricool gives you more actionable insights per dollar than almost any competitor.

## When Glow Social Is the Better Choice

Glow Social makes more sense if you:

- **Don't run paid social campaigns** — ad analytics don't help
- **Don't have time to analyze data** — a dashboard you never check adds no value
- **Don't create content** — analytics on zero posts is meaningless
- **Don't care about competitor analysis** — you just want your page active
- **Want social media handled, not optimized** — good enough beats perfect

**Bottom line:** Analytics tools only help if you're already posting. If the problem is getting posts out the door, data won't solve it.
