Med spas should post content that builds confidence: treatment education, provider authority, realistic expectations, reviews, aftercare tips, before-and-after proof with permission, and booking reminders.

## 30 Med Spa Post Ideas

1. Who a treatment is best for.
2. What to expect at the first appointment.
3. Provider introduction.
4. Review highlight.
5. Aftercare checklist.
6. Myth about injectables or skincare.
7. Before-and-after with permission.
8. Treatment timeline.
9. Seasonal skincare tip.
10. Product recommendation.
11. Common FAQ.
12. Consultation reminder.
13. Safety or credential post.
14. Difference between two services.
15. Recovery timeline.
16. Maintenance schedule.
17. New service announcement.
18. Team education update.
19. Booking availability.
20. Client concern explanation.
21. Treatment prep checklist.
22. Skin type education.
23. Package or membership reminder.
24. Event or open house post.
25. Local service-area post.
26. Provider quote.
27. "Save this" skincare tip.
28. Google review post.
29. Transformation story without overpromising.
30. Clear call-to-action for consultations.

## Weekly Mix

Use one education post, one proof post, one provider credibility post, and one booking reminder each week.
