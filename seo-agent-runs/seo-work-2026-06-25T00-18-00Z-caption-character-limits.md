## Do not overlearn if weak

If CTR does not improve, do not assume the title/snippet bet is wrong immediately. Possible causes include Google rewriting the snippet/title, official platform/help-center results satisfying the query directly, zero-click SERP behavior for simple numeric answers, and the short measurement window after a title change. Check the live rendered title/snippet and page-specific query data before changing the page again.
