## Republish checklist for each Priority 1 page

1. Update title and meta description.
2. Update H1 only when it improves query match and still reads naturally.
3. Add or move a direct-answer block into the first 150 words.
4. Update CTA language to preview-first:
   - Use `See posts from your website first`.
   - Avoid `automatic publishing`, `5-minute setup`, `fully automated`, and `content calendar`.
5. Add 3-5 internal links from the relevant cluster.
6. Add 2-3 links out to relevant sibling pages.
7. Update publish date or last-modified date.
8. Request indexing for Priority 1 pages in Search Console.
