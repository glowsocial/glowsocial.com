# Glow Social New Site Launch Content Bank

This folder is a **page-first content system** for the next Glow site.

It is designed to do four jobs at once:

1. give the new site immediate publishing inventory
2. keep SEO/AEO topics organized by cluster and buyer intent
3. make email/social adaptation easier later
4. reduce the chance that good ideas die inside one-off outbound

## Structure

- `roadmap/100-post-roadmap.md` — the full two-year content bank map
- `taxonomy.md` — cluster logic, buyer-intent model, and CTA rules
- `ops/publishing-sequence.md` — suggested cadence and release order
- `ops/publish-ready-tiers.md` — tiered edit/publish queue for all 100 drafts
- `ops/boomp-drops-operating-model.md` — how the 100-post bank becomes a two-year weekly Boomp Drops system
- `ops/boomp-drops-100-issue-map.md` — all 100 posts repackaged as weekly Boomp Drops issues
- `ops/boomp-drops-editing-rules.md` — editorial rules for making the drafts sharper, more specific, and less obviously AI
- `templates/` — reusable markdown templates for posts and email adaptation
- `templates/boomp-drops-email-template.md` — issue-level email template for weekly Drops
- `drafts/` — the drafted canonical pages, organized by cluster

## Default workflow

1. pick a topic from the roadmap
2. create or refine the canonical website post
3. adapt that post into email
4. pull 3–5 social cuts from the same core idea
5. publish and internally link into the relevant cluster

## Current status

- 100 mapped topics across 10 clusters
- 100 drafted posts ready for editing/publishing
- templates for canonical posts, email adaptations, and issue-level Boomp Drops emails
- an operating model + 100-issue map for turning the bank into two years of weekly Boomp Drops

## Notes

- These drafts are intentionally **website-first** and **preview-CTA aware**.
- The content leans toward Glow's strongest buyer-intent framing: owners do not want to carry the content burden, but they still need the business to look active and trustworthy.
