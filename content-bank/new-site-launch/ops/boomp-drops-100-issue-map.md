# Boomp Drops 100-Issue Map

This file repackages the 100-post content bank into a **100-issue Boomp Drops newsletter/archive plan**.

## Core rule
- Each roadmap post becomes **one weekly Boomp Drop issue**.
- The **canonical website page** is the source asset that gets indexed on the new site.
- The **email** is the adaptation layer, not the source of truth.
- 100 issues = roughly **100 weeks** of publishing, which is just under **two years** with a weekly cadence.

## Fields
- **Issue** — weekly drop number
- **Cluster** — thematic bucket for archive organization and internal linking
- **Intent** — buyer-intent angle for sequencing
- **Canonical slug** — the website-first asset to publish/index
- **Email angle** — the subject/angle to adapt into the newsletter issue
