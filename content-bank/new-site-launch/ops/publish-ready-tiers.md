# Publish-Ready Tiers

This file upgrades the 100-post bank from a raw draft inventory into a **practical editing and publishing queue**.

## Tier definitions

### Tier 1 — publish first
- Closest to Glow's strongest buyer-intent framing
- Best fit for launch, first-wave publishing, and core internal-link clusters
- Should be edited to final voice first, then turned into email/social adaptations

### Tier 2 — publish after the foundation is live
- Valuable, but less urgent than the first-wave pages
- Often better once Tier 1 pages exist to link into
- Includes stronger backlog, support pages, seasonal expansion, and authority depth

## Summary

- **Tier 1:** 34 posts
- **Tier 2:** 66 posts
- **Recommended editing order inside Tier 1:** owner burden → trust signals → content sources → buyers guides → local visibility → website engine → objections / proof / AI support
