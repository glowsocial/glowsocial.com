# How to Build a Content Library That Feeds Search and AI Discovery

Build pages that answer different questions at different stages.

A strong content library helps both search engines and AI tools because it gives them more useful material to surface, summarize, and connect back to the business.

## What a useful library usually includes
It often includes:
- service pages
- FAQ pages
- comparison pages
- trust and proof pages
- seasonal timing pages
- plain-language educational pages

That range creates more entry points.

## Why this works
Not every buyer searches the same way.

Some ask direct service questions. Some compare options. Some want reassurance. A library lets the business meet more of those paths without cramming everything into one page.

## Next step
If your site has only a few broad pages, expand into a tighter content library built around real questions and real decisions.

That usually improves both search reach and AI discoverability.
