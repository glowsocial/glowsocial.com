# How to Build a Repeatable Monthly Posting Rhythm

## Direct answer
Build the month from recurring categories, not endless reinvention.

A good monthly rhythm usually mixes reminders, proof, FAQs, service timing, and a few seasonal angles in a pattern the team can repeat without needing a brand-new idea universe every four weeks.

## What a simple rhythm can include
A practical month might include:
- one or two reminder posts
- one proof post
- one FAQ or explainer
- one seasonal timing post
- one reusable evergreen piece

That is enough for a steady baseline.

## Why this works
Repetition is not the enemy if the categories stay useful.

The customer is not seeing your system the way you are. They are just seeing whether the business looks current and helpful.

## Next step
If monthly planning feels chaotic, simplify the pattern before you try to generate more ideas.

A calmer rhythm usually lasts longer.
