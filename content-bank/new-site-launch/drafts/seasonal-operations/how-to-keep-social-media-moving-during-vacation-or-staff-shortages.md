# How to Keep Social Media Moving During Vacation or Staff Shortages

Lower the standard before the system breaks.

When staffing is thin, the goal is not to maintain your most ambitious content plan. The goal is to keep a believable baseline moving without creating more chaos for the team.

## What helps most
During lean stretches, simpler content usually wins:
- reminders
- review posts
- FAQ-based posts
- lightweight proof
- reused evergreen pieces
- clear seasonal timing posts

That is enough to keep the business from looking abandoned.

## Why this works
A quieter but steady presence is usually better than disappearing and then trying to overcorrect later.

Customers mostly want signs that the business is still active and responsive.

## Next step
If vacation season or staffing gaps are coming, plan the lighter baseline early.

That usually works better than pretending the normal rhythm will somehow survive unchanged.
